Rune Pouch Overview

The rune pouch is a small pouch that holds up to 16,000 of each of three rune types (48,000 runes total). It's considered essential for virtually every activity in OSRS because it frees up 3 inventory slots that would otherwise be occupied by rune stacks. Whether you're bossing, questing, or PKing, the rune pouch is a must-have item.

There are two versions of the rune pouch: the basic rune pouch and the divine rune pouch. The divine version can store 6 rune types instead of 3 and is created by combining a rune pouch with a thread of eldritch.

How to Get the Rune Pouch

There are two primary methods to obtain the rune pouch in OSRS:

Method 1: Slayer Reward Shop (Most Common)

Purchase the rune pouch from any Slayer master for 750 Slayer points. This is the most straightforward method but requires accumulating enough points through completing Slayer tasks.

Method 2: NPC Contact Spell (Alternative)

Cast the NPC Contact spell on the Mage of Zamorak to receive the rune pouch. This method requires completion of Lunar Diplomacy and the Lunar spellbook, but is free after the initial quest investment.

NPC Contact Spell Unlock

The NPC Contact method is worth understanding because it provides an alternative path to the rune pouch. Here's how to unlock and use it:

Required Quests:

  1. Lunar Diplomacy — unlocks the Lunar spellbook
  2. Dream Mentor — unlocks the NPC Contact spell

Using NPC Contact:

  1. Switch to the Lunar spellbook at the Lunar Isle altar
  2. Cast NPC Contact (requires 67 Magic, 2 astral runes, 2 cosmic runes, 2 law runes)
  3. Select the Mage of Zamorak from the contact list
  4. Speak to him and request the rune pouch
  5. He will give you a rune pouch for free

Note: This method can only be used once. If you lose the rune pouch, you'll need to buy a replacement from the Slayer shop or use NPC Contact again. The NPC Contact method is particularly useful for Ironmen who want to save their Slayer points for other rewards.

How the Rune Pouch Works

Using the rune pouch is straightforward:

  1. Fill it: Use runes on the rune pouch to store them. You can store up to 3 different rune types (or 6 with the divine rune pouch).
  2. Use runes: When you cast spells, runes stored in the pouch are automatically used. You don't need to withdraw them first.
  3. Check contents: Right-click the pouch and select "Check" to see what's stored inside.
  4. Empty it: Right-click and select "Empty" to remove all runes. They'll go to your inventory or bank if your inventory is full.

Important Notes:

  • The rune pouch is kept on death in most situations (protected as a low-value item)
  • Runes in the pouch are NOT lost on death — they stay in the pouch
  • You cannot store combination runes in the basic rune pouch
  • The divine rune pouch CAN store combination runes
  • You cannot have multiple rune pouches simultaneously

Rune Pouch Tips

  • ✅ Get the rune pouch as early as possible — it's arguably the best quality-of-life item in OSRS
  • ✅ Use a fire battlestaff — eliminates the need for fire runes, freeing a pouch slot
  • ✅ Keep the pouch in your inventory at all times — it's too useful to leave in the bank
  • ✅ Top up runes at the bank — check your pouch regularly so you don't run out mid-trip
  • ❌ Don't store rarely-used runes — maximize the value of each slot with your most-used runes
  • ❌ Don't forget the divine rune pouch upgrade — 6 slots is a massive improvement
  • ✅ Save up for 750 points before spending on other rewards — the pouch is that important
