OSRS How to Make Money with Crafting Low Level 2026

Money Making Crafting Low Level Updated 2026

Crafting can be a surprisingly strong money-maker in OSRS, even at relatively low levels. Battlestaves, dragonhide bodies, and gems all offer consistent margins that can turn a moderate investment into steady profit. This guide covers the best Crafting money-making methods for players who are not yet at 99 Crafting, with full profit margin breakdowns and practical tips.

Crafting as a Money Maker

Unlike most skills where you train for XP and the money is incidental, Crafting can be deliberately leveraged for profit by buying raw materials and crafting them into items with a higher Grand Exchange value. The key is knowing which items have reliable margins and volume on the GE.

  • Always check current GE prices before committing to a method — margins shift daily.
  • Higher Crafting levels unlock better profit-per-hour methods.
  • Crafting combines well with Slayer — many slayer monsters drop the materials for profitable crafts.
  • Some methods (battlestaves) are limited by daily stock from NPCs — plan accordingly.
  • The Crafting Guild chest and Nardah shop restock useful crafting supplies.

Battlestaves — Best Mid-Level Money Maker

Crafting battlestaves by adding orbs to battlestaves is one of the most popular and consistent money-making methods in OSRS. Orbs (air, water, earth, fire) can be charged via the Mage Training Arena or bought from the GE, then combined with battlestaves (purchased from Zaff's shop in Varrock) to create staffs worth significantly more than their combined input costs.

Battlestaves Method Summary

  • Requirements: Level 30 Crafting (air), 43 (water), 58 (earth), 62 (fire)
  • Profit/hr: ~200,000 – 600,000 GP depending on volume and method
  • XP/hr: ~100,000 – 200,000 Crafting XP
  • Bottleneck: Daily stock of battlestaves from Zaff is limited (number increases with Varrock Diary completion)
  • Action required: Buy battlestaves daily, combine with orbs, sell to GE

Dragonhide Bodies — Fast Craft, Strong Margins

Crafting dragonhide bodies requires cutting dragonhide into vambraces first (or buying them), then crafting bodies. The highest-value version is the Blue Dragonhide Body, which can be crafted from level 71 Crafting. At lower levels, green dragonhide is a reasonable alternative.

Dragonhide Bodies Method Summary

  • Green D'hide Body: Level 63 Crafting — Profit ~100–300 GP per body
  • Blue D'hide Body: Level 71 Crafting — Profit ~200–500 GP per body
  • Red D'hide Body: Level 77 Crafting — Profit ~200–400 GP per body
  • Black D'hide Body: Level 84 Crafting — Best XP, variable profit
  • XP per body: 186 (green) to 258 (black)
  • Speed: Up to 2,000 crafts per hour (very fast)

Sapphire Rings — Low Level Entry Point

Crafting sapphire rings is accessible from level 20 Crafting and was historically one of the first money-making methods available to low-level players. While the margins are thin, they provide both Crafting XP and a small profit, making them useful for budget players training Crafting while also wanting income.

Sapphire Ring Method Summary

  • Requirements: Level 20 Crafting, gold bar + sapphire + ring mould
  • Input cost per ring: ~700–900 GP
  • Output value: ~750–950 GP
  • Profit per ring: ~50–100 GP (thin margins)
  • Crafts/hr: ~1,800 – 2,000
  • XP per ring: 40
  • Best for: Low-level players who need Crafting XP and a small profit simultaneously

Other Low-Level Crafting Money Makers

Uncut Gem Cutting

  • Cut uncut gems (sapphires, emeralds, etc.) into cut gems for modest profit.
  • Profit margins: ~200–500 GP per gem depending on type.
  • Very fast crafting action — up to 3,000 cuts per hour.
  • Required level: 1 (opal) through 67 (onyx).

Glassblowing

  • Craft molten glass into orbs, vials, or lantern lenses using the glassblowing pipe.
  • Orbs are particularly profitable when combined with battlestaves.
  • Seaweed and sand can be obtained for free via Fossil Island or Zanaris.
  • Requires level 12 Crafting for empty orbs, 87 for light orbs.

Crafting Money Making Tips

  • Always check GE prices with a price-checking site before bulk buying — margins change weekly.
  • Buy Zaff's battlestaves every day — it is a consistent free profit that takes less than 5 minutes.
  • Use the Crafting Guild chest (level 40 Crafting, brown apron) to buy gold ore and craft jewellery for XP and profit.
  • High-alching crafted items (like rune items from Smithing + Crafting combos) can provide additional value.
  • The Crafting Cape perk gives unlimited teleports to the Crafting Guild — convenient for batching jewellery.
  • For maximum profit at 99 Crafting, consider crafting zenyte jewellery (items from Zulrah and Demonic Gorillas combined) — the highest value craftable jewellery in the game.
