OSRS How to Increase Slayer Points Fast 2026

Skill Training Slayer Updated 2026

Slayer points are the currency used to unlock crucial Slayer rewards including block slots, the Slayer helmet, and key upgrades. Accumulating them efficiently requires knowing which Slayer masters to use and when, how to leverage the streak bonuses, and which tasks to skip vs complete. This guide covers the fastest point farming strategies available in 2026.

How Slayer Points Work

Slayer points are awarded every time you complete a Slayer task. The amount varies by master and whether you are on a task streak. Key mechanics:

  • Every 10th task completion gives bonus points (multiplied reward).
  • Every 50th task gives even larger bonus points.
  • Higher-tier Slayer masters give more points per task.
  • Tasks can be skipped (costs 30 points) or blocked (costs 100 points).
  • Completing 1,000 tasks gives the "Slayer Veteran" achievement but no direct point bonus.

Best Slayer Masters for Points

Konar — Best Points Per Task (Mid-Game)

  • Gives 18 points per task — the highest baseline of any master.
  • Located at Mount Karuulm, requires 75 Slayer.
  • Tasks are location-specific (e.g., "kill 15 Blue Dragons in Taverley Dungeon"). This limits efficiency but the point reward is excellent.
  • Best for: Players with 75–85 Slayer who want fast point accumulation.

Duradel — Best Points for High-Level Players

  • Gives 15 points per task (lower than Konar).
  • Assigns the best high-level Slayer tasks (abyssal demons, drakes, dragonfire variants).
  • Located in Shilo Village — requires 100 combat and completion of Shilo Village quest.
  • Best for: Players 85+ Slayer who are also training combat and want best XP alongside points.

Nieve/Steve — Balanced Option

  • 12 points per task, accessible at 85 combat.
  • Located in Gnome Stronghold.
  • Assigns similar task list to Duradel, slightly fewer difficult tasks.
  • Best for: Players who want a blend of fast tasks and decent points without Shilo Village requirements.

The Optimal Point Farming Strategy

The fastest way to accumulate Slayer points uses a technique called "9+1 boosting" — completing 9 tasks with Turael (fast, trivial tasks worth 0 points each) then doing the 10th task with a high-tier master to collect the bonus streak reward.

9+1 Streak Farming Method

  1. Get assigned a task from Duradel (or Konar).
  2. If you want to farm points rather than complete that task, cancel it and take 9 trivial tasks from Turael.
  3. Complete all 9 Turael tasks as fast as possible (chickens, bats, goblins — all take 1–3 minutes each).
  4. Return to Duradel (or Konar) for your 10th task. You will receive the 10th task streak bonus.
  5. Complete that one task for the bonus points.
  6. Repeat: 9 Turael + 1 Duradel, over and over.

When to Skip vs Block Tasks

Once you have enough points, strategically blocking bad tasks dramatically improves the quality of your future task list, saving more points in the long run.

Universal Block Recommendations

  • Block: Cave crawlers (slow, venomous, bad XP)
  • Block: Gargoyles (if using Duradel/Nieve and you hate the fight)
  • Block: Desert Lizards (terrible XP, slow kills with ice cooler mechanic)
  • Block: Banshees (low XP, tedious)
  • Skip: Any task you cannot yet kill efficiently (spend 30 points)

Best Tasks to Boost (Never Skip)

  • Abyssal Demons (great XP + Abyssal Whip drops)
  • Hellhounds (fast XP, Smouldering Stone, Clue scrolls)
  • Black Demons (can cannon, good XP)
  • Dark Beasts (high Slayer requirement but excellent XP + drops)
  • Dagannoth (good XP, Kings access if high level)

Slayer Point Tips

  • Always complete the 10th, 50th, and 100th task at the highest-tier master available.
  • Buy the Slayer helmet as your first major purchase — the combat bonus saves time on every future task.
  • Use Turael reset carefully — it resets your streak, so time your cycles to avoid losing a 50th or 100th task bonus.
  • Konar gives Brimstone keys on task kills — these unlock Konar's chest with extra GP drops, making Konar tasks overall the most profitable beyond just points.
  • At 99 Slayer, the cape perk allows you to receive two tasks at once from any master, doubling your streak-building efficiency.
