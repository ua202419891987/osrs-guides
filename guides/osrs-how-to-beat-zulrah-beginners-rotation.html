Gear Setup

Budget Mage Setup:

  • Helmet: Mystic hat
  • Body: Mystic robe top
  • Legs: Mystic robe bottom
  • Weapon: Trident of the Seas
  • Shield: Mage's book or Tome of Fire
  • Boots: Mystic boots
  • Gloves: Barrows gloves (recommended) or combat bracelet
  • Amulet: Occult necklace (if available) or amulet of fury
  • Ring: Seers ring (i)

Budget Range Setup:

  • Helmet: Archer helm
  • Body: Black d'hide body
  • Legs: Black d'hide chaps
  • Weapon: Rune crossbow + Diamond bolts (e)

  • Amulet: Amulet of fury
  • Ring: Archers ring (i)

💡 Trident of the Seas is the most important upgrade for Zulrah — prioritize getting it before your first kill attempt.

Inventory Setup

Your inventory should be split between supplies and gear switches:

  • 8–10 Prayer potions or Saradomin brews
  • 2–3 Super restores
  • 5–8 Sharks or Anglerfish (high healing food)
  • Rune pouch (with Zulrah's boss teleport runes)
  • Zul-andra teleport scroll (for escaping)
  • Ring of recoil (optional, absorbs some damage)

The 4 Zulrah Rotations Explained

Zulrah always follows one of 4 fixed rotations. Each rotation begins the same way (Zulrah emerges in green/mage form on the west side), then diverges. Learn to identify which rotation you're in based on the first 2 phases.

Phase-by-Phase Beginner Guide

Starting Position:

Always stand on the east side of the island when Zulrah first appears. Face west. This gives you the widest positioning options for all rotations.

Green Phase (Mage Attacks):

Use your Ranged gear. Activate Protect from Magic prayer. Zulrah stands still in green form — this is your DPS window. Shoot/attack until it dives.

Blue Phase (Range Attacks):

Switch to Mage gear. Activate Protect from Ranged prayer. Move to avoid the toxic clouds Zulrah spawns.

Red Phase (Mage + Range Combo — Jad Phase):

This is the hardest phase. Watch which attack Zulrah uses first: green projectile = protect Magic; pink projectile = protect Ranged. Switch prayer immediately when the attack changes.

Beginner Tips for Zulrah

  • ✅ Practice rotations on a safe-death world first
  • ✅ Use the Zulrah rotation helper tool (websites exist for this)
  • ✅ Don't panic — if you die, Zulrah has a gravestone mechanic to recover items
  • ✅ Start with 1 kill at a time until you're comfortable with all phases
  • ✅ Use Zul-andra teleport scrolls to exit between kills if needed
  • ✅ Prayer flicking (switching between Protect Magic/Ranged) reduces potion usage dramatically
