Rune Spinning Overview

Rune spinning refers to the process of spinning flax into bowstrings using a spinning wheel. Bowstrings are in constant demand because Fletchers need them to string bows — specifically yew longbows and magic longbows, which are popular alch items. This creates a reliable market for bowstrings.

Each flax is spun into one bowstring. The process requires level 1 Crafting and can be done at any spinning wheel in the game. The key to maximizing profit is choosing the spinning wheel closest to a bank.

Profit Per Hour Calculation

The number of bowstrings you can spin per hour depends on your method and location. Here are realistic numbers based on 2026 GE prices:

Manual Spinning (Clicking Each Flax):

At Seers' Village with optimal banking, you can spin approximately 1,200–1,500 bowstrings per hour by manually clicking each flax. This is tedious but requires no setup.

Using Keyboard Shortcuts:

By using the spacebar to spin and number keys to bank, experienced players can reach 1,600–1,800 bowstrings per hour.

Alternative Spinning Methods

While flax-to-bowstring is the most popular spinning method, there are alternatives worth considering:

Spinning Wool into Balls of Wool

Balls of wool sell for 30–50 GP each and require shearing sheep. This is more of a F2P method since the profit is lower than bowstrings. Wool spinning gives 2.5 Crafting XP per ball — much less than bowstrings.

Spinning Magic Roots into Magic Strings

At level 19 Crafting, you can spin magic roots into magic strings. These sell for more than bowstrings but the supply of magic roots is limited, making this impractical for volume spinning.

Spinning Sinew into Crossbow String

Sinew from cooked meat can be spun into crossbow strings at level 10 Crafting. These sell for around 100–150 GP, comparable to bowstrings, but obtaining sinew is slower than picking flax.

Tips to Maximize Profit

  • ✅ Use Seers' Village spinning wheel — it's the closest to a bank and saves the most time per trip
  • ✅ Buy flax in bulk — buy 25,000+ flax at once so you never run out mid-session
  • ✅ Use the spacebar to spin — it's faster than clicking the spinning wheel each time
  • ✅ Check GE prices before starting — bowstring margins can fluctuate
  • ❌ Don't pick flax if you value time — buying flax is almost always more efficient
  • ❌ Don't use Lumbridge spinning wheel if you're a member — Seers' is significantly faster
  • ✅ Combine with Fletching — string your own bows for even more profit per bowstring
