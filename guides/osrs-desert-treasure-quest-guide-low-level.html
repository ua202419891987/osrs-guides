OSRS Desert Treasure Quest Guide for Low Level Players 2026

🧩 Quest Guide🔮 Ancient MagicksUpdated 2026

Desert Treasure unlocks Ancient Magicks — one of OSRS's most powerful spellbooks. Complete it as a low-level player with this optimized guide that minimizes risk against the 4 tough bosses in 2026.

Pre-Quest Preparation for Low Levels

  • ✅ Bring Prayer potions (10+ minimum) — you'll need Protect prayers for all 4 bosses
  • ✅ Bring Ice gloves (from Hero's Quest miniquest) for Fareed boss
  • ✅ Bring Super restore potions — Kamil lowers your stats with ice magic
  • ✅ Stamina potions — running from Kamil's ice attacks is critical
  • ✅ Combat level 60+ recommended; 75+ makes bosses significantly easier
  • ✅ Use Magic for all bosses (or Ranged with Ruby bolts (e) if you have access)

Boss 1: Dessous the Vampire

🗺️ Location: Graveyard, Morytania💀 Danger: High🛡️ Prayer: Protect from Melee

Dessous can hit 50+ with melee. Stand behind a gravestone and use Magic or Ranged. He's weak to air spells (Wind wave recommended). Bring 4+ prayer potions — this fight can be long for low-level players. Low level tip: Use snare/entangle spells to freeze him, then attack from distance.

Boss 2: Kamil the Ice Troll King

🗺️ Location: Ice Path, Trollheim💀 Danger: Very High🛡️ Prayer: Protect from Melee + Warm clothing

Kamil lowers your stats and freezes you with ice attacks. The ice path leading to him is dangerous — bring warm clothing (Santa hat, Bruma torch, or light sources). Use Fire spells (Fire Blast minimum) for maximum damage. Restore stats with super restore potions after each freeze.

Boss 3: Fareed the Fire Warrior

🗺️ Location: Smoke Dungeon, Pollnivneach💀 Danger: Medium🛡️ Prayer: Protect from Melee + Ice Gloves EQUIPPED

Fareed disarms your weapon if you don't wear Ice gloves. Equip them! Use Water magic (Water Blast/Water Wave) for double damage. He's the easiest boss if you have Ice gloves equipped.

Boss 4: Damis the Shadow Hound

🗺️ Location: Shadow Dungeon, Baxtorian Falls💀 Danger: High🛡️ Prayer: Protect from Melee

Damis drains Prayer rapidly — bring lots of prayer potions (8+). He has 2 forms. The first form is slow, use snare/entangle spells on him. Second form is faster and hits harder. Use your best combat style. Low levels struggle most with this boss — consider bringing an inventory full of Prayer potions + food if you're below 70 combat.

Rewards & Ancient Magicks Overview

Completing Desert Treasure unlocks the Ancient Magicks spellbook, which includes:

  • Ice spells (Ice Burst, Ice Barrage) — freeze enemies, essential for bursting Slayer tasks
  • Blood spells (Blood Burst, Blood Barrage) — heal yourself by damaging enemies, great for PvM
  • Shadow spells — lower enemy Attack/Strength/Defence (situational)
  • Smoke spells — poison enemies (less commonly used)

💡 Desert Treasure is a game-changing quest for PvM, Slayer, and PvP. Worth doing as early as your stats allow.
