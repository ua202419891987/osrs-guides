Entry Requirements & Setup

Chambers of Xeric is located on Mount Quidamortem in the Zeah region. There are no hard skill or quest requirements to enter, but having adequate stats and gear is essential for completing raids efficiently.

Recommended Stats:

  • Attack/Strength/Defence: 80+ (melee)
  • Ranged: 80+ (essential for Olm)
  • Magic: 80+ (essential for Olm)
  • Prayer: 70+ (Piety and Rigour/Augury)
  • Herblore: 78+ (for overloads inside raids)

How CoX Loot Works

Loot in Chambers of Xeric is awarded based on personal points. The more points you accumulate during the raid, the higher your chance of receiving a unique drop and the better your common loot will be. Points are earned by dealing damage to rooms and the Great Olm.

Points System:

  • Each room contributes points based on your damage dealt
  • Great Olm (final boss) gives the majority of points
  • Dying reduces your points by 30–40%
  • Team size affects individual point thresholds

The unique drop rate scales with total team points. A solo player with 30,000+ personal points has approximately a 1/24 chance of seeing a unique drop. In larger teams with higher total points, the rate improves but the unique is randomly assigned to one player.

CoX Profit Tips

  • ✅ Maximize personal points — more points means better common loot and higher unique chance
  • ✅ Don't die — death costs 30–40% of your points, dramatically reducing loot
  • ✅ Learn to 4:1 or 3:0 Olm — efficient Olm cycles are the biggest point source
  • ✅ Use overloads — they boost your damage significantly throughout the raid
  • ❌ Don't join masses if you want maximum GP — small teams or solo are more profitable per hour
  • ❌ Don't skip scout — certain room layouts are much faster and more profitable
  • ✅ Learn entry mode first — practice mechanics before committing to normal mode
