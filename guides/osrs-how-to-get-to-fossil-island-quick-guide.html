OSRS How to Get to Fossil Island Quick Guide 2026

🗺️ Transport 📜 Quest 🦕 Fossil Island Updated 2026

Fossil Island is one of the most useful areas in OSRS, home to birdhouse trapping, hardwood trees, and the Fossil Island noteable features. This quick guide explains how to unlock and get to Fossil Island in 2026, including the Bone Voyage quest requirement and all transport methods.

Requirements to Access Fossil Island

Before you can set foot on Fossil Island, you must complete the Bone Voyage quest. This quest has the following requirements:

Quest Requirements:

  • Dragon Slayer I — must be completed
  • The Dig Site — must be completed

Skill Requirements:

SkillLevel RequiredNotes
Agility15For some obstacles during the quest
Construction10Not strictly required but helpful
Crafting10Needed during the quest
Herblore10Needed for a potion during the quest
Woodcutting10Needed for building a raft

These are very low requirements, making Fossil Island accessible to most players early in their account progression.

Bone Voyage Quest Walkthrough

Bone Voyage is a relatively short quest that serves as the gateway to Fossil Island. Here's a quick summary of the steps:

  1. Speak to the Curator in the Varrock Museum after completing The Dig Site
  2. Talk to the barge foreman north of the Dig Site
  3. Speak to the Lead Navigator and the Bone Voyage crew
  4. Gather the required items: 3 oak planks, 1 rope, 1 copper wire
  5. Repair the barge and set sail for Fossil Island
  6. Upon arrival, help the museum establish a base camp
  7. Complete the fossil display activities to finish the quest

The quest takes approximately 15–30 minutes and rewards you with permanent access to Fossil Island via the barge and other methods.

The barge is the default method but is slow because it requires running from the Dig Site to the barge each time. Mushtree teleport is the recommended method for regular visits once you've unlocked the network.

Mushtree Teleport Network

Fossil Island features a Mushtree teleport network similar to the fairy ring system. Once you activate each mushtree, you can teleport between them freely. There are five mushtree locations:

  • Mushroom Meadow — central hub, near birdhouse areas
  • Seaweed Swamp — near seaweed and anglerfish spots
  • Verdant Valley — near hardwood trees and herb patches
  • Forest — near the Ancient Cavern entrance
  • Hilltop — near the bank and general store

Each mushtree must be activated once by visiting it on foot and clicking "Activate." After that, you can teleport to it from any other mushtree. The Hilltop mushtree is the most useful since it's next to the bank.

Activities on Fossil Island

Fossil Island offers a wide range of activities that make it one of the most important areas in the game:

Birdhouse Trapping

The primary reason most players visit Fossil Island. Birdhouses provide passive Hunter XP and can be checked every 50 minutes. With 4 birdhouse spots on the island, this is one of the best daily activities in OSRS.

Hardwood Trees

Teak and mahogany trees grow on Fossil Island, providing Woodcutting XP and valuable logs for Construction training. These are the only freely available hardwood trees in the game outside of the Woodcutting Guild.

Fossil Displays

Unidentified fossils found on the island can be cleaned and added to displays in the Varrock Museum for Prayer and Slayer XP. This is a unique passive training method.

Underwater Section

The underwater area on the northeast side of the island offers seaweed harvesting, sand crab killing, and lobster/crab fishing. Access requires a diving apparatus from the quest.

Fossil Island Tips

  • ✅ Unlock all mushtrees immediately — run to each one and activate it for fast future travel
  • ✅ Set up birdhouse runs early — they provide excellent passive Hunter XP
  • ✅ Use the Hilltop mushtree — it's closest to the bank for depositing items
  • ✅ Bring a digsite pendant — fast teleport to the barge area
  • ❌ Don't skip Bone Voyage — Fossil Island is too valuable to ignore
  • ❌ Don't ignore hardwood trees — teak and mahogany are excellent for Construction
  • ✅ Collect unidentified fossils — clean them for free Prayer and Slayer XP
