God Wars Dungeon Overview

God Wars Dungeon (GWD) is located north of Trollheim and requires 60 Strength or 60 Agility to access the boulder at the entrance. You also need a rope on your first visit. Inside, four factions battle for supremacy: Bandos, Armadyl, Zamorak, and Saradomin.

To enter any boss room, you must first kill 40 followers of that faction. This kill count can be done in the dungeon's main area before entering the boss room. Once inside the boss room, the boss spawns every 90 seconds along with three minions.

Soloing General Graardor (Bandos)

General Graardor is the Bandos boss and is the most popular solo GWD target. He uses melee attacks exclusively, making Protect from Melee effective. His three minions use melee, ranged, and magic respectively.

Solo Bandos Strategy:

  1. Get 40 Bandos kill count by killing goblins, ogres, or orks
  2. Enter the Bandos boss room with Protect from Melee active
  3. Attack General Graardor while praying melee against his attacks
  4. Kill the ranged minion (Steelwill) first — his magic attacks hit through prayer
  5. Then kill the melee minion (Strongstack) and ranged minion (Spinepillar)
  6. Pick up drops, eat food as needed, and wait for the next spawn

With mid-level gear (Bandos chestplate/ tassets or Barrows), you can expect 2–4 kills per trip. Each kill takes approximately 1–2 minutes depending on your gear and stats.

Soloing Kree'arra (Armadyl)

Kree'arra is the Armadyl boss and attacks with ranged and magic. She cannot be attacked with melee — you must use ranged or magic against her. This makes Armadyl more gear-intensive since you need a good ranged setup.

Solo Armadyl Strategy:

  1. Get 40 Armadyl kill count by killing spiritual rangers and mages
  2. Enter the Armadyl boss room with Protect from Ranged active
  3. Use ruby bolts (e) and diamond bolts (e) with an Armadyl crossbow or dragon crossbow
  4. Kill Kree'arra while avoiding her tornado special attack
  5. Kill the mage minion (Wingman Skree) first to reduce damage
  6. Pick up drops and repeat

Solo Armadyl is generally slower kills per trip than Bandos due to Kree'arra's high defence and the need for expensive ranged ammunition. However, Armadyl unique drops are worth more on average.

Solo GWD Tips

  • ✅ Bring a bonecrusher — automatically buries bones, saving inventory space
  • ✅ Use Guthan's for healing — weapon-swap to Guthan's to heal on minions between kills
  • ✅ Bring imbued heart — boosts magic level for better blood barrage healing
  • ✅ Get EC ridge for faster trips — extended antivenom and super combat potions
  • ❌ Don't fight without protection prayers — GWD bosses hit extremely hard
  • ❌ Don't forget kill count — you need 40 KC each time you re-enter
  • ✅ Start with Bandos — it's more forgiving and cheaper to learn
