OSRS How to Unlock the Abyss Guide 2026

📜 Miniquest 🔮 Runecrafting ⚠️ Wilderness Updated 2026

The Abyss is a dangerous but incredibly useful area that provides fast access to every Runecrafting altar in OSRS. This guide covers how to unlock the Abyss through the Mage of Zamorak miniquest, how to use it effectively for Runecrafting, the risks involved, and the abyssal creatures you'll encounter in 2026.

What is the Abyss?

The Abyss is a mysterious dimension accessible through the Wilderness. It contains rifts to every Runecrafting altar in the game, making it the fastest way to craft runes. Instead of running to each altar individually, you enter the Abyss and click the corresponding rift to teleport directly to the altar.

The catch? The Abyss is located in the Wilderness (level 5–6), meaning you can be attacked by other players. Additionally, the Abyss drains your prayer points upon entry and contains aggressive creatures. This creates a risk-reward dynamic that every Runecrafter must weigh.

Mage of Zamorak Miniquest

Before you can use the Abyss, you must complete the Mage of Zamorak miniquest. This is a short and easy task:

Requirements:

  • Rune Mysteries quest completed
  • No combat requirements (but bring food for the Wilderness)

Step-by-Step:

  1. Go to the Varrock Wilderness ditch, north of the city
  2. Just north of the ditch (Wilderness level 4–5), find the Mage of Zamorak
  3. Speak to him — he'll tell you about the Abyss and the Zamorakian cult
  4. He asks you to speak to him again in Varrock at the Chaos Temple
  5. Go to the Chaos Temple in southern Varrock (south of the zamorak church)
  6. Speak to the Mage of Zamorak there
  7. He'll explain the Abyss and offer to teleport you there
  8. Accept the teleport — you'll arrive in the Abyss
  9. Explore the Abyss briefly and find your way to the center (use the "exit" option)
  10. Return to the Mage of Zamorak to complete the miniquest

After completion, the Mage of Zamorak will permanently teleport you to the Abyss when you speak to him in the Wilderness. You can also use the Abyss directly by entering the rift in the Wilderness.

Using the Abyss for Runecrafting

Once unlocked, the Abyss becomes your primary Runecrafting method. Here's how an efficient Abyss run works:

Abyss Runecrafting Loop:

  1. Start at the Edgeville bank with a full inventory of pure essence
  2. Equip a pickaxe (for mining the rock obstacle) and charged glory amulet
  3. Run north into the Wilderness and enter the Mage of Zamorak's teleport
  4. You appear in the outer ring of the Abyss
  5. Navigate to the inner ring by passing through one of the obstacles (mine, chop, burn, or distract)
  6. In the inner ring, click the rift corresponding to the rune you want to craft
    7. <7>You're teleported to the altar — craft your runes
  7. Use your charged glory to teleport back to Edgeville
  8. Bank and repeat

Obstacle Types in the Outer Ring:

The fastest method is using the Gap (Agility) or Tendrils (Thieving) obstacles, as they have the quickest animations. Mining the rock is also popular since most players have a high Mining level.

Risk vs Reward Analysis

The Abyss is in the Wilderness, which means PKers can attack you. Here's a realistic risk assessment:

Risks:

  • PKer attacks — players may attack you while running to the Mage of Zamorak or inside the Abyss
  • Prayer drain — entering the Abyss drains ALL your prayer points
  • Skulling — attacking another player in the Abyss skulls you
  • Abyssal creatures — aggressive monsters can damage you

Risk Mitigation:

  • Wear cheap gear — only risk what you can afford to lose
  • Never carry more than 1 glory and 1 pickaxe
  • Keep pure essence noted or in small stacks
  • Use the Protect Item prayer before entering (saves your most valuable item)
  • World hop if you see PKers near the Mage of Zamorak

The risk is relatively low because you should never carry expensive items into the Wilderness. Your worst-case loss is a charged glory, a pickaxe, and some essence — typically under 30K GP total. The reward of 500K+ GP per hour far outweighs the occasional PK death.

Abyssal Creatures

The outer ring of the Abyss is populated by aggressive creatures. While they're not dangerous to prepared players, they can be a nuisance:

These creatures are mostly a distraction. They can hit small amounts of damage and drain your run energy, but they won't kill a prepared player. The real threat in the Abyss is other players, not the monsters.

Abyssal Demons (Not in the Abyss)

Despite the name, abyssal demons are NOT found in the Abyss. They're found in the Slayer Tower and require 85 Slayer. They drop the abyssal whip, one of the most iconic weapons in OSRS. Don't confuse the two locations!

Abyss Tips

  • ✅ Wear a charged glory at all times — it's your escape route back to Edgeville
  • ✅ Use cheap gear only — graceful + glory + pickaxe is all you need
  • ✅ Use the Gap obstacle — it's the fastest way through the outer ring
  • ✅ Keep multiple glories in the bank — you'll burn through charges quickly
  • ❌ Don't carry expensive items into the Wilderness — PKers target well-geared players
  • ❌ Don't panic when you see a PKer — run south toward Edgeville, not deeper into the Wild
  • ✅ Do abyss runs on less populated worlds — fewer PKers means safer runs
