Ironman-Specific Drops

Sarachnis is especially valuable for Ironmen because several drops are hard to obtain elsewhere without buying from the GE:

Sarachnis Cudgel

A crush weapon with +65 crush attack and +62 crush strength. For Ironmen, this is one of the best crush weapons available before the inquisitor's mace. It's particularly useful for killing gargoyles and other crush-weak monsters. The cudgel has a drop rate of approximately 1/500.

Gear Setup for Ironmen

Sarachnis attacks with ranged and magic, so you need gear that defends against both. Since she's not aggressive with melee, you can safely use melee to fight her:

Budget Melee Setup:

  • Head: Helm of Neitiznot or Berserker helmet
  • Body: Torag's platebody or Bandos chestplate
  • Legs: Torag's platelegs or Bandos tassets
  • Weapon: Abyssal whip or Sarachnis cudgel (crush)
  • Shield: Dragon defender
  • Boots: Dragon boots

Ranged Setup (Alternative):

Using ranged with Protect from Magic is viable if you have Karil's crossbow or Armadyl crossbow. However, melee is generally faster kills due to Sarachnis's lower crush defence.

Kill Strategy & Mechanics

Sarachnis has a few mechanics you need to understand for efficient kills:

Basic Attacks:

  • Ranged attack — green projectiles; use Protect from Missiles
  • Magic attack — blue projectiles; use Protect from Magic
  • She alternates between styles — switch prayers accordingly

Special Attack — Web Spawn:

Sarachnis periodically spawns spiderlings and web traps. The spiderlings deal small damage but can stack. The web traps hold you in place for a few seconds. When you see web graphics on the ground, step away immediately.

Prayer Switching:

The key to efficient Sarachnis kills is watching her attack animation and switching prayers. Green splat = ranged (pray ranged), blue splat = magic (pray magic). With good prayer switching, you take minimal damage per kill.

Sarachnis Ironman Tips

  • ✅ Fight Sarachnis for seeds — it's one of the best seed sources for Ironmen
  • ✅ Use crush weapons — Sarachnis is weak to crush; the cudgel is perfect here
  • ✅ Bring alch runes — many drops are worth alching for extra GP
  • ✅ Learn prayer switching — reduces food consumption dramatically
  • ❌ Don't ignore spiderlings — they can stack damage if you let them live
  • ❌ Don't use slow weapons — Sarachnis has relatively low HP, so speed matters
  • ✅ Combine with farming runs — plant the seeds you get between trips
