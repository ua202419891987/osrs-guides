OSRS Killing Green Dragons Money Per Hour 2026

Money Making Combat Wilderness Updated 2026

Green dragons in the Wilderness are one of the best entry-level combat money makers in OSRS, offering 400,000–800,000 GP per hour for mid-level accounts. Green dragonhide and dragon bones are both high-demand items. However, the Wilderness location means PKers are a constant danger. This guide covers the optimal setup, locations, kill rates, and strategies to stay alive while maximising your profit.

Why Kill Green Dragons?

Green dragons are popular money makers because they require moderate combat stats to kill efficiently, drop two high-value items every kill (green dragonhide and dragon bones), and respawn quickly. Their Wilderness location keeps the area semi-dangerous, which reduces competition compared to safe alternatives.

  • Each kill drops 1 green dragonhide (~1,500 GP) and 1 dragon bones (~2,500 GP).
  • Average loot per kill: ~4,000–5,000 GP including rare drops.
  • 100–150 kills per hour is achievable with proper setup.
  • Total GP/hr: ~400,000–800,000 depending on kill speed and world choice.
  • No high combat level required — 60+ in Attack, Strength, Defence is sufficient.

Green dragons use both Melee and Fire Breath attacks. Antifire potions combined with an Anti-dragon shield (or Dragonfire shield) provide full protection against fire breath. Without this combo, every fire breath deals significant damage.

Best Green Dragon Locations

Wilderness Locations

  • East Green Dragons (Wilderness, ~17–19 Wilderness): Best all-around spot. Several dragons grouped together, teleport accessible (below level 20). Easy escape route.
  • West Green Dragons (Wilderness, ~10–15 Wilderness): Less popular, slightly safer Wilderness depth but fewer dragons per area.
  • Graveyard of Shadows (~17 Wilderness): More dragons, more competition, but excellent respawn density. Slightly higher PK risk from passersby.

The East Green Dragon location (around level 17–19 Wilderness) is generally the best choice — it is below the Teleblock threshold of level 20, meaning you can always teleport out to safety when a PKer appears. This dramatically reduces your risk.

Non-Wilderness Alternative: Myths' Guild

After completing Dragon Slayer II, you can access the Myths' Guild basement which contains multiple green dragons in a safe (non-Wilderness) environment. Kill rates are slightly lower due to the layout, but there is zero PK risk.

Gear Setup for Green Dragons

Budget Setup (Bring to Wilderness)

  • Helm: Rune full helm or Berserker helm
  • Body: Rune platebody
  • Legs: Rune platelegs
  • Weapon: Rune scimitar or Dragon scimitar
  • Shield: Anti-dragon shield
  • Boots: Rune boots
  • Gloves: Combat bracelet or barrows gloves
  • Amulet: Amulet of strength or fury
  • Ring: Wealth or berserker ring

Recommended Inventory

  • Antifire potion (4) × 2 — full fire breath protection
  • Prayer potion (4) × 2 — sustain Protect from Melee
  • Lobsters or sharks × 10–15 for healing
  • Looting bag — doubles your GP per trip in the Wilderness
  • Emergency teleport: Ring of Dueling or teleport runes

Anti-PK Strategy

  • Stay below level 20 Wilderness — You can teleport freely below level 20, which is your most important escape mechanism.
  • Watch your minimap constantly — Red dots appearing on minimap mean other players are approaching.
  • Teleport immediately when a player enters your area — Do not wait to see if they are friendly.
  • Use low-value gear — Never bring your best items. If you die, your loss should be minimal.
  • Avoid high-population worlds — Less busy worlds mean fewer PKers and less competition for dragon spawns.
  • Bring a looting bag but keep it full for quick banking — Overloaded inventory means slow movement and easier PKing.
  • Log out if a PKer appears and you cannot teleport — The logout timer is 10 seconds in combat; click the door or move away first.
