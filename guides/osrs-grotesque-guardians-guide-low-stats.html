⚔️ Boss Guide 💀 Slayer 🆕 Low Stats Updated 2026

Requirements & Unlocking

To fight the Grotesque Guardians, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Slayer level 75 — required to receive Gargoyle tasks
  • Active Gargoyle Slayer task — you can only fight them while on task
  • Broken roofing tile — used to access the Slayer Tower roof (one-time use per trip)
  • Completion of Priest in Peril — required to access the Slayer Tower area

Dawn & Dusk Phase Mechanics

The Grotesque Guardians fight consists of two bosses that you fight sequentially. Understanding their mechanics is essential for low-stats kills:

Dawn (First Phase):

  • Uses ranged and magic attacks
  • Summons burning gargoyles that chase you — avoid them or they deal heavy damage
  • Uses a special attack where she flies up and drops fire — run to avoid the orange tiles
  • At 50% HP, she gains increased attack speed

Dusk (Second Phase):

  • Uses melee attacks exclusively — Protect from Melee is highly effective
  • Occasionally uses a rock throw attack — cannot be prayed against
  • Uses a wing attack that pushes you back — stay close to avoid it
  • At 50% HP, he enters an enrage phase with faster attacks

You must kill Dawn first before Dusk becomes vulnerable. Once Dawn dies, Dusk absorbs her power and attacks more aggressively. This transition is the most dangerous part of the fight for low-stats players.

Low-Stats Kill Strategy

With lower stats, survival is more important than speed. Here's the recommended approach:

Dawn Phase:

  1. Equip your melee gear and attack Dawn with Protect from Missiles active
  2. When she summons burning gargoyles, run away and let them despawn
  3. When she flies up, watch for orange tiles and run to a safe spot
  4. Use shark or better food to sustain through her ranged attacks
  5. When she reaches 50% HP, switch to Protect from Magic if her magic attacks hit harder

Dusk Phase:

  1. Immediately switch to Protect from Melee
  2. Stand close to Dusk to minimize his rock throw attacks
  3. When he does his wing flap, step back one tile then step forward again
  4. At 50% HP, eat more aggressively as his attack speed increases
  5. Use your special attack weapon (dragon claws or DDS) to burst him down in the enrage phase

Grotesque Guardians Tips

  • ✅ Always fight on a Gargoyle task — you cannot access the boss without one
  • ✅ Use a Slayer helmet (i) — the 16.67% damage boost is massive
  • ✅ Prioritize avoiding burning gargoyles — they deal more damage than Dawn herself
  • ✅ Bring house tabs — teleport to your POH for quick banking between kills
  • ❌ Don't fight without Super Combat potions — they make a huge difference at low stats
  • ❌ Don't stand in the fire — Dawn's fire drop attack can hit 30+ damage
  • ✅ Save Slayer points for cornerstone tiles — they save inventory space vs brittle tiles
