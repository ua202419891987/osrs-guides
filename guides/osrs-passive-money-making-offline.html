Method 1: Managing Miscellania (Kingdom Management)

📊 Profit: 200,000–400,000 GP/day (passive) ⚡ Req: Throne of Miscellania quest 🗺️ Location: Miscellania Island

Managing Miscellania is the single best passive income source in OSRS. After completing the quest, you assign workers to collect resources while you're offline.

Setup Steps:

  1. Complete Throne of Miscellania quest (moderate length)
  2. Complete Royal Trouble quest to double worker capacity (highly recommended)
  3. Deposit 750,000–1,000,000 GP into the Kingdom coffers
  4. Maintain approval rating at 100% (do tasks daily — takes 2 minutes)
  5. Assign workers to: Maples (best GP), Coal, or Herbs (for ironmen)

💡 2026 Tip: With Royal Trouble completed, your Kingdom generates value roughly every 45 minutes. Log in once per day to collect for maximum efficiency.

Method 2: Herb Farming Runs

📊 Profit: 200,000–800,000 GP per run ⚡ Req: Level 32+ Farming 🗺️ Location: Multiple patches

Herb runs take about 5 minutes of active play every 80 minutes. Plant herbs, log off, come back when they're grown, harvest and sell on the GE.

Best Herbs to Plant in 2026:

  • Ranarr Weed (level 32) — ~6,000–8,000 GP per herb, easiest profit herb
  • Snapdragon (level 62) — ~7,000–10,000 GP per herb
  • Torstol (level 85) — ~8,000–12,000 GP per herb, best endgame

Patch Locations (5 patches for maximum efficiency):

  1. Falador (south of party room)
  2. Ardougne (east of market)
  3. Catherby (southeast)
  4. Canifis (requires Priest in Peril)
  5. Farming Guild (requires 65 Farming)

Use Supercompost (or Ultracompost) to reduce death chance and increase yield.

Method 3: Birdhouse Trapping on Fossil Island

📊 Profit: 100,000–200,000 GP per run ⚡ Req: Level 5 Hunter + Bone Voyage quest 🗺️ Location: Fossil Island

Birdhouses are placed every 50 minutes and yield bird nests (containing seeds, rings, or gems). This takes 2–3 minutes per run and generates passive income while the birds fill the traps.

Birdhouse Profit Breakdown:

  • Each birdhouse gives 5–10 nests
  • Nests contain seeds (herb seeds, tree seeds) worth 500–50,000 GP each
  • Magic birdhouses (level 75 Hunter) are most profitable

💡 Combine birdhouse runs with herb runs for a 5-minute passive routine that earns 300,000–1,000,000 GP per run at high levels.

Method 4: GE Flipping While Offline

The Grand Exchange processes buy/sell orders while you're logged out. Set up buy orders before going offline and sell orders when you return. This requires capital but generates pure passive income.

How to Flip While Offline:

  1. Research items with stable buy/sell spreads (use GE tracker tools)
  2. Place buy offers below current market price before logging off
  3. When you return, your items should be bought
  4. Relist at sell price and go offline again
