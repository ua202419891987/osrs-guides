Levels 1–60: Birdhouse Runs + Fast Start

Birdhouse Runs (Level 5+)

📊 XP: 4,000–18,000 per run⚡ Req: Level 5 Hunter + Bone Voyage🗺️ Location: Fossil Island

Complete Bone Voyage quest, then set up birdhouses at the 4 birdhouse spots on Fossil Island. Each birdhouse gives massive XP when collected after 50 minutes. Do a run every time you log in — it's the most efficient passive Hunter XP in the game.

Levels 60–80: Maniacal Monkeys

📊 XP: 60,000–110,000/hr⚡ Req: Level 60 Hunter + Monkey Madness II🗺️ Location: Kruk's Dungeon, Ape Atoll

Maniacal monkeys are the best AFK Hunter method. After Monkey Madness II, place a box trap in the monkey pit in Kruk's Dungeon. The monkeys continuously destroy/re-trigger traps, requiring only occasional adjustments. Bank monkey tails for decent GP.

Levels 80–99: Herbiboar

Herbiboar on Fossil Island gives the best Hunter XP in the game at 140K–180K/hr. Track the boar through fossil paths, using herbs you track it to indicate the next direction. Each catch gives herbs (ranarr, snapdragon, torstol) — making this profitable AND great XP.

Birdhouse Run Routine

Combine birdhouse runs with herb runs every 50 minutes. Each run takes 2–3 minutes and gives 4,000–18,000 XP (depending on birdhouse tier). Over the course of 1–99, birdhouses alone can provide 3,000,000+ passive Hunter XP.
