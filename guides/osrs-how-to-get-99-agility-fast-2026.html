OSRS How to Get 99 Agility Fast 2026

Skill Training Agility Updated 2026

Agility is one of the grindiest skills in OSRS, but with the right courses and strategies you can cut hours off the journey to 99. This guide covers the fastest training methods from level 1 to 99, including optimal course progression through Seers Village, Pollnivneach, and Ardougne, along with advice on farming Marks of Grace for the Graceful outfit.

Why Train Agility?

Agility is a core quality-of-life skill in OSRS. Higher Agility levels mean faster run energy restoration, access to shortcuts across the game world, and the ability to complete certain quests and areas. At 99 Agility, your run energy regenerates nearly twice as fast as at level 1.

  • Run energy restores faster at higher Agility — crucial for bossing and skilling.
  • Agility shortcuts unlock faster travel routes across the entire game.
  • Graceful outfit (collected via Marks of Grace) reduces weight and dramatically increases run energy regeneration.
  • Required for various quests and diary tasks including the elite Ardougne Diary.
  • Agility level affects the chance of your pouches degrading in Runecrafting.

Levels 1–30: Early Courses

The early Agility levels go quickly regardless of course. Push through these as fast as possible — the XP rates improve dramatically once you can access the Pollnivneach or Seers Village courses.

Early Level Progression

  1. Level 1–10: Gnome Stronghold Agility Course — Located inside the Tree Gnome Stronghold. Low XP but the only option at level 1. Takes about 20 minutes to level 10.
  2. Level 10–20: Draynor Village Agility Course — Slightly better XP. Accessible from Draynor Village bank.
  3. Level 20–30: Al-Kharid Agility Course — Better XP again. Located north-east of Al-Kharid bank.
  4. Level 30–40: Varrock Agility Course — Good XP for the level range. Located in north Varrock near the Museum.
  5. Level 40–52: Canafis Agility Course — First course with meaningful Marks of Grace spawns. Requires Priest in Peril quest access.
  6. Level 52+: Seers Village or Pollnivneach — The real grind begins.

Seers Village Agility Course (Level 60+)

The Seers Village Agility Course is one of the most popular mid-level courses due to its high XP rates and the availability of Marks of Grace. To maximise efficiency, unlock the Camelot teleport and move the respawn point to Seers Village bank via the Kandarin Elite Diary or use a Camelot teleport after each lap.

Seers Village Course Summary

  • Level Required: 60 Agility
  • XP/hr: ~40,000 – 55,000
  • Laps/hr: ~55 – 70
  • XP per lap: 570
  • Marks of Grace: Good spawn rate
  • Tip: Use Camelot teleport to skip back to start — saves 5–10 seconds per lap

With Kandarin Hard Diary complete (requires 70 Agility), the Seers Village bank becomes the respawn point for the Camelot teleport, saving substantial time each lap. This alone increases XP rates by roughly 10%.

Pollnivneach Agility Course (Level 70+)

The Pollnivneach Rooftop Course is one of the best XP courses in the game once you reach level 70. It gives excellent XP per hour and very high Marks of Grace generation — making it the best place to collect Marks if you have not yet completed the Graceful outfit.

Pollnivneach Course Summary

  • Level Required: 70 Agility
  • XP/hr: ~50,000 – 60,000
  • Marks of Grace/hr: ~14 (highest rate of any course)
  • XP per lap: 890
  • Location: Pollnivneach village, accessible via magic carpet
  • Best for: Players who want both XP and Marks of Grace simultaneously

Ardougne Agility Course (Level 90+)

The Ardougne Rooftop Course is unlocked at level 90 and provides excellent XP per hour along with Marks of Grace. It is the best standard course for levels 90–99 in terms of both XP and mark collection.

Ardougne Course Summary

  • Level Required: 90 Agility
  • XP/hr: ~55,000 – 62,000
  • XP per lap: 793
  • Marks of Grace/hr: ~8
  • Best for: Levels 90–99 for consistent marks and good XP

Marks of Grace — Graceful Outfit Farming

Marks of Grace appear randomly on course tiles while training Agility. They are used to purchase the Graceful outfit from Grace in the Rogues' Den. A full Graceful outfit costs 260 Marks of Grace total and reduces run energy drain while improving recovery speed.

Collect Marks at Pollnivneach (level 70–90) for the highest mark-per-hour rate. Priority: get the full outfit before level 80 if possible, as it dramatically reduces the number of Stamina Potions needed.

Agility Tips

  • Always pick up Marks of Grace — they disappear after 10 minutes if uncollected.
  • Stamina Potions restore run energy — use them aggressively to maintain running throughout laps.
  • Wearing Graceful set bonuses drastically reduces Stamina Potion usage.
  • Completing Kandarin Hard Diary moves the Camelot teleport destination to Seers bank, saving ~5 seconds per lap.
  • The Summer Garden (Gnome Restaurant reward) gives excellent AFK Agility XP but requires Prifddinas access.
  • Hallowed Sepulchre (at level 52+) gives faster XP than rooftop courses but requires intense concentration.
  • Lap streaks at some courses (like Canafis) give slight bonus XP for completing multiple consecutive laps.
