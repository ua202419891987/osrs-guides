OSRS Low Gear Vorkath Guide for Beginners 2026

👹 Boss Guide 💰 2M+ GP/hr Updated 2026

Vorkath is OSRS's most consistent money-making boss at 2–4M GP/hour. This guide covers how to kill Vorkath efficiently with budget gear in 2026, including the Woox Walk technique and all attack patterns.

Requirements for Vorkath

  • Dragon Slayer II quest — required to fight Vorkath after tutorial
  • Ranged 75+ (90+ recommended)
  • Prayer 70+ (74 for Rigour recommended)
  • Defence 70+

Budget Gear Setup

Ranged Gear (Budget):

  • Helmet: Slayer helmet (i) or Void knight helm
  • Body: Void knight top or Black d'hide body
  • Legs: Void knight robe or Black d'hide chaps
  • Weapon: Rune crossbow or Dragon hunter crossbow (best-in-slot)
  • Shield: Dragonfire ward or Anti-dragon shield (REQUIRED)
  • Ammo: Diamond bolts (e) or Ruby bolts (e)
  • Amulet: Amulet of fury or Necklace of anguish
  • Boots: Pegasian boots or Ranger boots
  • Ring: Archers ring (i)

💡 Dragon Hunter Crossbow gives 30% damage bonus and 30% accuracy against dragons — it's the biggest DPS upgrade for Vorkath. Costs ~50M GP but pays for itself within 50 kills.

⚠️ CRITICAL: You MUST have an Anti-dragon shield or Dragonfire ward. Without it, Vorkath's dragonfire attack will one-shot you regardless of your HP.

The Woox Walk (Acid Phase)

During the acid phase, Vorkath shoots rapid-fire attacks while pools spawn under your feet. The Woox Walk lets you deal damage while avoiding the pools.

How to Woox Walk:

  1. When acid phase starts, click one tile south of your position
  2. Immediately click your crossbow to attack Vorkath
  3. Repeat: move one tile south, click attack — back and forth
  4. This keeps you moving (avoiding acid) while still attacking

💡 Practice this without pressure first. Even clicking one tile away and waiting (no attack) is better than standing still in acid.
