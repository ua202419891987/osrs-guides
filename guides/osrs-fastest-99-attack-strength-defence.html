Levels 1–60: Early Game Training

Sand Crabs (Level 1–60)

📊 XP: 20,000–60,000/hr⚡ Req: None🗺️ Location: Hosidius Beach, Kourend

Sand crabs are the classic low-level AFK training spot. They have 60 HP, hit very low, and are aggressive for 10+ minutes before you need to run away and reset aggro.

Waterfall Quest Rush (Instant Level 30)

Complete Waterfall Quest before any combat training — it awards 13,750 Attack and Strength XP, instantly taking you from 1 to 30 in both skills. No combat required. Takes 30 minutes.

Levels 60–85: Mid Game

Ammonite Crabs (Level 60–85)

📊 XP: 60,000–80,000/hr⚡ Req: Bone Voyage quest🗺️ Location: Fossil Island

Ammonite crabs are the upgraded version of sand crabs — 100 HP, super aggressive, and constantly packed with training players. Bring your best weapon and AFK for 10 minutes at a time.

Nightmare Zone (NMZ) — Best Mid Game

NMZ provides the most consistent AFK melee XP in the game. Customize easy bosses (Count Draynor, Tree Spirit, Kendal, Me, Khazard Warlord) for maximum AFK potential. With Dharok set at 1 HP + Rock Cake, you can achieve 90,000+ XP/hr at 70+ melee stats.

Levels 85–99: Fastest Methods

Nightmare Zone (Dharok Method)

📊 XP: 120,000–150,000/hr⚡ Req: 70+ Strength, Dharok set🗺️ Location: Yanille NMZ

Dharok's set amulet damage scales with lower HP. Use a rock cake/absorptions to stay at 1 HP and repeatedly spec with Dharok hits for massive XP. Each hit can land for 70+, translating to 400+ XP per hit.

💡 2026 Update: The newly introduced Sulphur Nagua monsters (Varlamore Part 2) offer competitive XP rates (110K–150K/hr) with better loot than NMZ, making them a viable alternative for players who prefer active training.

Melee Gear Progression

  • ✅ Level 60–70: Abyssal whip (Attack) / Saradomin sword (Strength)
  • ✅ Level 70+: Abyssal whip (Attack), Ghrazi rapier (BiS), Osmumten's fang (high-Def bosses)
  • ✅ Strength training: Saradomin sword → Dragon hunter lance (dragons) → Soulreaper axe (BiS)
  • ✅ Always use Berserker ring (i) + Amulet of fury → Torture necklace for max DPS
