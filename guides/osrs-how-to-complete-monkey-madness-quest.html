OSRS How to Complete Monkey Madness Quest 2026

📜 Quest 🐒 Monkey Madness ⚔️ Combat Updated 2026

Monkey Madness is one of the most iconic quests in OSRS, granting access to the dragon scimitar and the island of Ape Atoll. This complete guide covers all requirements, a step-by-step walkthrough, the Jungle Demon boss fight, and quest rewards for 2026.

Quest Requirements

Monkey Madness has several prerequisite quests that must be completed before you can start:

Required Quests:

  • Tree Gnome Village — grants access to the Gnome Stronghold
  • The Grand Tree — introduces the gnome storyline
  • Tree Gnome Stronghold — access to the gnome area
  • Rune Mysteries — needed for teleportation

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Part 1: Gnome Stronghold & King Narnode

  1. Speak to King Narnode Shareen in the Tree Gnome Stronghold
  2. He tells you about the 10th squad that went missing on Ape Atoll
  3. Agree to help find the squad
  4. Speak to Daero in the hangar to travel to Ape Atoll

Part 2: Crash Island & Ape Atoll

  1. After the crash landing, speak to Garkor on the island
  2. Learn about the monkeys' oppressive rule on Ape Atoll
  3. Travel to Ape Atoll via the boat
  4. Be careful — the monkey archers on Ape Atoll will attack you on sight
  5. Make your way to the temple area while avoiding or protecting from monkey guards

Part 3: Monkey Gree Gree & Disguise

  1. Speak to Zooknock in the dungeon beneath the temple
  2. He will help you create a monkey gree gree (monkey disguise)
  3. You need a monkey talisman and monkey bones/ corpse
  4. Kill a monkey (Karamja monkeys work) for the bones
  5. Return to Zooknock with the items to receive the gree gree
  6. Wield the gree gree to transform into a monkey — this lets you walk freely on Ape Atoll

Part 4: Infiltration & Signal

  1. With the gree gree equipped, enter the monkey village
  2. Speak to the Monkey King Awowogei in the palace
  3. Attend the monkey ceremony in the temple
  4. Give the signal to Garkor when the timing is right
  5. Prepare for the Jungle Demon boss fight

Jungle Demon Boss Fight

The Jungle Demon is the final boss of Monkey Madness, combat level 195. It attacks with magic and melee, and can hit hard if you're not prepared.

Recommended Setup:

  • Food: 10–15 sharks or better
  • Prayer: Protect from Magic (primary) + Piety if available
  • Weapon: Dragon scimitar or better (melee), or ranged weapon
  • Armour: Barrows gear or better
  • Potions: Super combat potion or combat potions

Fight Strategy:

  1. Enter the demon's arena and immediately turn on Protect from Magic
  2. Attack with melee while maintaining protect from magic
  3. The demon has two special attacks: a magic blast and a ground slam — both are blocked by prayer
  4. Keep your prayer points high — bring prayer potions (4–6)
  5. Eat when below 40 HP — the demon can combo hit through prayer occasionally

Jungle Demon Fight Stats

Demon Combat Level195

If you die, you can recover your items from the arena. The demon does not heal, so you can return and continue the fight. Alternatively, you can safe-spot the demon using ranged by standing behind the pillars in the arena.

The dragon scimitar is the most significant reward. It requires 60 Attack to wield and provides excellent accuracy and strength bonuses, making it the best scimitar before the dragon defender combo. For many players, this weapon serves as their primary melee weapon until they can afford an abyssal whip.

Monkey Madness Tips

  • ✅ Get the gree gree as early as possible — it makes navigating Ape Atoll infinitely easier
  • ✅ Bring antipoison — some traps on Ape Atoll can poison you
  • ✅ Safe-spot the Jungle Demon — use pillars to block melee attacks if your combat stats are low
  • ✅ Bring plenty of prayer potions — you'll need them for the boss fight
  • ❌ Don't walk around Ape Atoll without the gree gree — the monkey guards will destroy you
  • ❌ Don't forget monkey bones — you need them for the gree gree quest step
  • ✅ Buy the dragon scimitar immediately after — it's a massive upgrade for 60 Attack players
