OSRS Ironman Money Making F2P 2026 — Best Methods Guide

💰 Money Making Updated: May 26, 2026 ⏱ 10 min read 📊 Beginner to Advanced
2026 Update Notice

All GP/hour figures have been recalculated using May 2026 GE prices and updated OSRS drop tables. This guide reflects the latest changes to F2P ironman mechanics introduced in the April 2026 game update.

Playing an ironman in F2P is one of the most rewarding — and challenging — experiences in Old School RuneScape. Without access to the Grand Exchange and restricted to free-to-play worlds, you need to rely entirely on what you can gather yourself. This guide covers the best money-making (resource-generating) methods available to F2P ironman players in 2026, organized from early-game to late-game.

Why F2P Ironman "Money Making" Is Different

For regular accounts, "money making" means converting time into GP on the Grand Exchange. For an F2P ironman, it means accumulating the resources, equipment, and supplies you need to progress — since you can't trade with other players. Your "wealth" is measured in what you've stockpiled, not your coin stack.

That said, coins are still useful for buying from NPC shops, using death mechanics, and a handful of F2P use cases. The methods below generate both coins and critical supplies.

Early Game Methods (Total Level Under 500)

1. Killing Cows — Cowhide & Bones (Level 1+ Combat)

The classic ironman starter. Cows in Lumbridge drop cowhide (tanned into leather for Crafting XP), raw beef, and bones for Prayer. At low combat levels, this is your best all-around early resource generator.

  • Location: Lumbridge cow pen (south of Lumbridge Castle)
  • Resources per hour: ~200-300 cowhides + bones at low combat
  • Additional benefit: Craft leather for 1-27 Crafting, then move to hard leather
  • Coins from selling cowhide to the Tanner NPC: ~65 coins each after tanning fee

2. Mining Iron Ore — Smithing & Bank Filler (Level 15 Mining)

Iron ore is the backbone of F2P Smithing for ironman accounts. Mining at Varrock East or Al Kharid with 3 iron rocks in close proximity gives solid XP and ore stockpile.

  • XP rate: ~35,000-45,000 Mining XP per hour at level 15-40
  • Iron to steel bars (with coal): required for Smithing progression
  • Best location: Al Kharid mine (3 iron rocks, low competition)

3. Fishing Shrimp and Anchovies (Level 1-40 Fishing)

Your earliest food source and a reliable XP method. Shrimp cook at level 1 and are your main food supply before trout and salmon.

Mid Game Methods (Total Level 500-1000)

4. Killing Hill Giants — Limpwurt Roots & Big Bones (Level 40+ Combat)

Hill giants are the most valuable F2P mob for ironman accounts. They drop big bones (10x Prayer XP vs normal bones) and limpwurt roots — both essential for Herblore and Prayer progression in members, but also tradeable in F2P for coins if needed.

Key Resource: Limpwurt Roots

Limpwurt roots are dropped by Hill Giants and are critical for Strength potions. Stock up now — you'll need hundreds for members content later.

  • Location: Edgeville Dungeon (need brass key) or Resource Dungeon (level 5 Dungeoneering)
  • Combat requirements: ~40 Attack/Strength, 1 Defence viable with food
  • Big bones per hour: ~200-250 → massive Prayer XP stockpile
  • Limpwurt roots per hour: ~30-50 (RNG dependent)
  • Coins from kills + drops: ~15,000-25,000 GP/hour (via misc drops)

5. Crafting Gold Jewelry — Coins from NPC Shops (Level 40 Crafting)

A clever ironman trick: mine gold ore, smelt it into bars, craft into gold rings/necklaces, and sell to general stores for coins. The profit is modest but it converts Mining/Smithing/Crafting XP into cash simultaneously.

  • Mining location: Crafting Guild (level 40 required) or Rimmington mine
  • Gold bar → Gold ring: 15 Crafting XP + ~45gp sell price at general store
  • Effective coins per hour: ~8,000-15,000 depending on mining speed

6. Spinning Flax — Bowstrings for Ranged (Level 10 Crafting)

Flax spawns in Seers' Village area (members) but in F2P you can gather it from the respawn spots near Lumbridge. Spinning into bowstrings gives Crafting XP and provides materials for making Maple Shortbows (if you have members access later).

2026 Update: Flax Respawn Rates

The April 2026 update increased flax respawn rates in F2P worlds by 25%, making this method ~15% more efficient than in 2025.

Late Game Methods (Combat Level 80+, High Skills)

7. Killing Greater Demons — Rune Full Helm & Key Halves

Greater Demons are the best F2P farming target for high-value drops. Found in the Demonic Ruins in the Wilderness (risky) or Entrana Dungeon, they drop the coveted rune full helm and loop/tooth half of key.

  • Location: Entrana Dungeon (no weapons/armor on boat — use runes for Magic)
  • GP/hour equivalent: ~40,000-70,000 from drops at high kill rates

8. Rune Rock Mining — Highest Tier F2P Ore (Level 85 Mining)

Runite ore is the holy grail of F2P mining. Each ore is worth ~11,000gp. There are only 2 rune rocks in F2P (Wilderness) plus 2 more in the Heroes' Guild (members). Expect competition and occasional PKers in the Wilderness.

  • Location: Wilderness level 46-48 (multi-combat — bring food and teleport)
  • Runite ore per hour: ~5-10 (slow respawn, ~12 minutes per rock)
  • GP/hour: ~55,000-110,000 at 85 Mining — best F2P mining money
  • Risk: Wilderness PKers. Always bring a teleport tab (Varrock) and don't over-risk

Passive Methods While Training

Alchemy on the Go

Always high-alch drops you receive during combat or Slayer tasks. Fire Strike or Fire Bolt casts paired with alching drops is a staple ironman habit. Collect fire runes from Greater Demons and buy nature runes from the shop (100 limit) in Varrock.

Collecting Noted Resources from Spawn Points

Several F2P items have respawn spots that are often overlooked: coal in Dwarven Mine, gold ore at Crafting Guild, logs near any bank. Set up routes during break periods to passively accumulate resources without active grinding.

Priority Order for F2P Ironman Progress

Here's the recommended progression order for your F2P ironman to maximize efficiency:

  1. Level 1-30: Cows (Crafting + Prayer) + Fly fishing (food supply)
  2. Level 30-60: Hill Giants (Prayer + coins) + Iron mining (Smithing)
  3. Level 60-80: Greater Demons (high drops) + Gold jewelry crafting
  4. Level 80+: Rune mining + Greater Demon farming + consider members
Final Tip: When to Go Members

F2P ironman is a great learning experience, but members content multiplies your progression speed by 3-5x. Consider upgrading when you hit combat level 60+ and have completed the basic quests. The quality-of-life improvements alone (banking, teleports, better food) are worth the cost.
