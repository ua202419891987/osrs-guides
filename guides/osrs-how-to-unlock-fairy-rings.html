OSRS How to Unlock Fairy Rings — Quick Guide 2026

🧩 Teleport Unlock 🧚 Fairy Tale Updated 2026

Fairy Rings are the most powerful teleport network in OSRS — over 40 destinations worldwide. Here's the fastest way to unlock them in 2026.

What Are Fairy Rings?

Fairy Rings let you teleport to over 40 locations across Gielinor using a simple 3-letter code. They're essential for efficient bossing, slayer, and general travel. Examples:

  • DKS — Dagannoth Kings
  • BKQ — Kalphite Queen
  • AIQ — Tzhaar (Fight Caves)
  • CIR — Feldip Hills (Zulrah prep)
  • AKQ — Piscatoris Fishing Colony

Quest Requirements for Fairy Ring Access

For Partial Access (Dramen Staff Required):

  • Complete Lost City quest — gives you the Dramen staff
  • Wield Dramen staff → click any fairy ring → full teleport access

For Full Access (No Staff Required):

  • Complete A Fairy Tale Part I — Growing Pains
  • Complete A Fairy Tale Part II — Cure a Queen (start only)
  • After beginning Part II, you can use fairy rings without the Dramen staff

Step 1: Complete Lost City Quest

Lost City Requirements:

  • Level 31 Crafting
  • Level 36 Woodcutting
  • No quest prerequisites

Lost City Walkthrough (Quick):

  1. Talk to the soldier near Lumbridge Swamp (east side, near Draynor Village)
  2. Head into Lumbridge Swamp, search the tree stump in the middle to find a leprechaun
  3. Ask about Zanaris → he tells you to cut the tree nearby with no axe equipped
  4. Cut the Tree (Spirit tree) with woodcutting — a Tree Spirit attacks you (bring food)
  5. Get the Dramen Branch from the tree
  6. Craft it into a Dramen Staff using the bank at Lumbridge (requires level 31 Crafting, no materials needed beyond the branch)
  7. Travel to Entrana (no weapons/armour allowed), enter the shed to access Zanaris
  8. Quest complete — you can now use Fairy Rings with Dramen staff equipped

Step 2: A Fairy Tale Part I — Growing Pains

This quest gives you the ability to start Part II. Requirements:

  • Level 17 Farming
  • Level 29 Herblore
  • Lost City completed
  • Nature Spirit completed

The quest involves helping the Fairy Godfather and fighting a plant boss. It's moderately long but straightforward.

Step 3: Start A Fairy Tale Part II

You don't need to complete Part II — just start it. Simply talk to the Fairy Godfather in Zanaris after completing Part I. Once you've started Part II, you can use Fairy Rings without equipping the Dramen staff.

💡 2026 Tip: Most veteran OSRS players do this as one of their first priorities. The freedom to use fairy rings without a staff equipped is a massive quality-of-life upgrade.
