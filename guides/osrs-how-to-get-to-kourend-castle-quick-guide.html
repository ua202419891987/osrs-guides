Why Kourend Matters

Great Kourend is a massive region on the western coast of Zeah. It's home to some of the most important content in OSRS, making it an area you'll visit frequently throughout your account progression. Here's why Kourend Castle and the surrounding area matter:

  • Five Houses — each offers unique content, shops, and favor rewards
  • Chambers of Xeric — the most profitable raid in OSRS is accessed through Kourend
  • Farming patches — the highest concentration of farming patches in any single area
  • Woodcutting Guild — one of the best WC training spots in the game
  • Mount Karuulm — home to the Alchemical Hydra and Karuulm Dungeon
  • Nightmare Zone — located just outside Kourend

Recommended Methods:

For quick visits: Kourend Castle Teleport is the best option if you have 69 Magic and are on the Arceuus spellbook. It teleports you directly to the castle courtyard.

For first-time access: Take the boat from Port Sarim after completing the Client of Kourend quest. This is the introductory method and is free.

For farming runs: Use fairy ring DJR or the Skills necklace to access specific farming areas quickly.

Notable Features & Content

Beyond the houses and farming, Kourend offers numerous other important features:

Chambers of Xeric (CoX)

Located on Mount Quidamortem in the northwest of Kourend. This is OSRS's first raid and one of the most profitable activities in the game. Access requires traveling to the mountain via Xeric's talisman or on foot.

Woodcutting Guild

Requires 60 Woodcutting and 75% Hosidius favor. Contains redwood trees, the highest-level tree in the game, plus a bank right next to the trees. This is the best Woodcutting training spot in OSRS.

Tithe Farm

A Farming minigame in Hosidius that provides excellent Farming XP and rewards including the farmer's outfit and grape seeds. Requires 34 Farming and 100% Hosidius favor.

Sand Crabs

Located on the Piscarilius coast, sand crabs are one of the best low-effort combat training methods in OSRS. They have low defence, high HP, and are aggressive — perfect for AFK melee training.

Nightmare Zone (NMZ)

Just north of Kourend, NMZ is a combat minigame where you fight bosses from quests you've completed. Great for melee XP and earning imbue scrolls for your rings and black mask.

Kourend Travel Tips

  • ✅ Unlock Kourend Castle Teleport early — it's the fastest way to reach the castle
  • ✅ Use fairy ring DJR for Hosidius — it lands you near the farming patches
  • ✅ Get Xeric's talisman for CoX trips — it provides fast access to Mount Quidamortem
  • ✅ Complete Client of Kourend first — it's required for all Kourend content
  • ❌ Don't walk from Port Sarim every time — unlock better teleport methods ASAP
  • ❌ Don't ignore house favor — the unlocks are extremely valuable
  • ✅ Incorporate Kourend into your farming runs — too many patches to skip
