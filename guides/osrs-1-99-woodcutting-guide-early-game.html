OSRS 1-99 Woodcutting Guide for Early Game 2026

Skill Training Woodcutting F2P Friendly Updated 2026

Woodcutting is one of the most approachable skills in OSRS, suitable for both free-to-play and members players. Whether you are just starting out or pushing for the cape, this guide walks you through every stage from level 1 to 99, including the optimal F2P route, the fastest members path through Oaks, Willows, Teaks, and Mahogany trees, and the beloved AFK Redwoods method for the final stretch.

Axe Upgrades — Always Use the Best Available

Your axe determines how quickly you chop logs. Always upgrade to the best axe your level allows. Axes can be wielded in the weapon slot (for +10% speed) or simply held in the inventory.

F2P Woodcutting Route (1–99)

Free-to-play players have fewer tree options but can still reach 99 efficiently. Here is the optimal F2P path:

F2P Route Summary

  1. Level 1–15: Normal Trees — Chop regular trees anywhere. Lumbridge courtyard has plenty. You need about 60 logs to reach level 15.
  2. Level 15–30: Oak Trees — Oak trees give 37.5 XP per log vs 25 for regular trees. Best F2P spot: Draynor Village or Varrock east bank.
  3. Level 30–60: Willow Trees — Willows give 67.5 XP per log and are the backbone of F2P Woodcutting. Best spot: Draynor Village willows by the river (very close to bank).
  4. Level 60–99: Yews or Willows — Yews give 175 XP but are slow and contested. Willows remain competitive for pure XP in F2P. For profit, cut Yews and sell logs.

Members Woodcutting Route (1–99)

Members have access to significantly more tree types and locations, enabling much faster XP rates. Here is the recommended members progression:

  1. Level 1–15: Normal Trees — Any location. 2–5 minutes of cutting.
  2. Level 15–30: Oak Trees — Draynor Village or Woodcutting Guild (requires level 60 and 75% Hosidius favour). About 30 minutes.
  3. Level 30–35: Willows — Draynor Village or Barbarian Outpost. Short stint.
  4. Level 35–65: Teaks — Ape Atoll (post-Monkey Madness) or Fossil Island. Best teak spot: Ape Atoll, where two teak trees grow close together.
  5. Level 65–99: Mahogany Trees — Ape Atoll (three mahogany trees). Requires 65+ and monkey greegree. Very fast XP at higher levels.

Teak Trees at Ape Atoll

  • Location: South-east corner of Ape Atoll island
  • Requirements: Monkey Madness I quest, monkey greegree
  • XP/hr at 60: ~65,000
  • XP/hr at 80: ~95,000
  • Logs banked: Yes, via Ape Atoll teleport + bank
  • Method: Cut two teak trees in rotation, bank when full

Mahogany Trees at Ape Atoll

  • Location: Ape Atoll (east side)
  • Requirements: Level 50 Woodcutting, Monkey Madness I
  • XP/hr at 65: ~80,000
  • XP/hr at 90: ~115,000
  • Logs banked: Optional (drop or bank)
  • Profit: Mahogany logs sell for ~500 GP each

Redwood Trees — Best AFK Method

Redwood trees in the Woodcutting Guild are the best AFK Woodcutting method in the game. The logs are very heavy, so you drop them rather than bank (unless you want profit). The trees have enormous amounts of HP, meaning they rarely deplete — you can AFK for up to 5 minutes without the tree falling.

Redwoods AFK Summary

  • Location: Woodcutting Guild, Great Kourend (requires 75% Hosidius favour)
  • Requirements: Level 90 Woodcutting
  • XP/hr: ~60,000 – 80,000
  • AFK window: Up to 5 minutes
  • Log value: ~700 GP each (modest profit if banked)
  • Best for: Players who want 99 with minimal attention from level 90

While Redwoods give less XP per hour than Teaks or Mahogany, the extremely high AFK window makes them a favourite for players who want to train while doing other tasks. Many players switch from Mahogany to Redwoods at level 90 purely for the convenience.

Woodcutting Tips

  • The Woodcutting Guild in Great Kourend provides a passive +7 Woodcutting boost while inside it.
  • Lumberjack outfit (obtained from Temple Trekking) grants up to 2.5% bonus Woodcutting XP.
  • Completing the Western Provinces Hard Diary unlocks the ability to cut Crystal trees, though this is more of a late-game activity.
  • For F2P players, Draynor Village willows next to the bank are the best overall XP spot until Yews are viable.
  • The Bird Nests that randomly drop while Woodcutting can contain seeds worth millions of GP — always pick them up.
  • Redwood locations in the Woodcutting Guild have two trees close together, minimising movement between respawns.
