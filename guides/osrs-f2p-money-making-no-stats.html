OSRS F2P Money Making No Stats Required 2026

💰 Money Making 🆓 Free-to-Play Updated 2026

New to OSRS on a free account? These methods require absolutely no skill levels — perfect for brand new players who need gold fast.

Method 1: Collecting Bones Near Lumbridge

📊 Profit: ~20,000–40,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: None 🗺️ Location: Lumbridge

Bones are used for Prayer training and always have demand. Kill goblins or cows near Lumbridge, collect every bone, and sell on the GE. Dragon bones are more valuable but require higher combat.

Best Bone Sources for Zero-Stat Players:

  • Goblin bones — abundant in Lumbridge Swamp
  • Chicken bones — Farmer Fred's farm just north of Lumbridge
  • Cow bones + hides — dual income from the cow farm east of Lumbridge

Method 2: Mining Rune Essence

📊 Profit: ~30,000–50,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: Rune Mysteries quest (very short) 🗺️ Location: Rune Essence Mine

Complete the Rune Mysteries quest (takes about 10 minutes) to unlock access to the Rune Essence Mine. Each rune essence sells for 15–25 GP, but you can mine very fast even at level 1 Mining.

Steps:

  1. Complete Rune Mysteries quest (talk to Duke Horacio in Lumbridge Castle)
  2. Teleport to the Rune Essence Mine via Aubury in Varrock
  3. Mine a full inventory and teleport back
  4. Repeat and sell in bulk on the GE

💡 Pro tip: Mine 10,000+ before selling — bulk sells faster and at better prices.

Method 3: Picking Up Bananas at Musa Point

📊 Profit: ~25,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: None (needs 30 GP for boat) 🗺️ Location: Karamja (Musa Point)

Bananas are used in farming and cooking. The banana plantation on Musa Point (Karamja) has unlimited banana trees. Pick bananas, bank at Port Sarim deposit box, repeat.

Method 4: Cutting Yew Logs (Level 60 Woodcutting)

📊 Profit: ~60,000–80,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: Level 60 Woodcutting 🗺️ Location: Varrock, Falador

Yew logs sell for 400–500 GP each. With a rune axe and level 60+ Woodcutting, you can cut 120–160 yews per hour. Best locations: Edgeville yews (close to bank) and Falador park.

This is the classic F2P money making method that works at higher levels. While not truly "no stats," the path from 1 to 60 Woodcutting only takes a few days of training.

F2P Money Making Tips

  • ✅ Start with cowhides + bones for dual income
  • ✅ Save up for a rune axe ASAP — it dramatically speeds up woodcutting
  • ✅ Sell in bulk on GE for better prices
  • ✅ Do Rune Mysteries quest on day 1 for essence mining access
  • ✅ Target 60 Woodcutting as your first major goal for consistent F2P income
