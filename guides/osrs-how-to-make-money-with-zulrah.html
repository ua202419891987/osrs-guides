OSRS How to Make Money with Zulrah 2026

Boss Guide Money Making Zulrah Updated 2026

Zulrah is one of the most lucrative bosses in OSRS, consistently earning 2–5 million GP per hour for experienced killers. The snake boss requires memorising rotation patterns and switching between gear styles, but once mastered it is extremely reliable money. This guide covers GP per kill, optimal kill rates to aim for, the value of Zulrah's scales, and tips for efficient looting.

Why Farm Zulrah?

Zulrah offers some of the most consistent GP in OSRS because every kill produces Zulrah's scales alongside magic and ranged supplies. The boss also has unique drops like the toxic blowpipe and serpentine helm worth tens of millions of GP. Even without those rare drops, the baseline loot makes every kill worth 50,000–100,000 GP on average.

  • Every kill drops 1–174 Zulrah's scales (avg ~75 scales, worth ~100 GP each = ~7,500 GP).
  • Consistent mage/ranged supplies make up most of the kill value.
  • Uniques (blowpipe, serpentine helm, tanzanite fang, magic fang, uncut onyx) are rare but extremely valuable.
  • No Slayer level required — accessible to any account with the quest done.
  • Kill rate (kills per hour) is the biggest factor in GP/hr — focus on improving rotations.

Understanding Zulrah's Scales

Zulrah's scales are the most consistent drop from every kill and are a major crafting ingredient for some of the best equipment in OSRS. They are used to create:

  • Toxic Blowpipe: Charged with scales — one of the best Ranged weapons in the game.
  • Serpentine Helm: Charged with scales — provides venom immunity.
  • Trident of the Swamp: The best mid-level Magic weapon, charged with scales.
  • Antivenom+ Potions: Require scales as an ingredient.

Because scales are consumed by these top-tier items, demand is always high. The price stays relatively stable around 100–130 GP per scale, making them a very predictable source of income.

Looting & Efficiency Tips

  • Always take all drops — even common drops like grapes and unfinished potions add up over 30+ kills per session.
  • The fastest banking route is using the Zul-andra teleport scroll (obtained from Zulrah drops) — this teleports directly to Zulrah's lair, allowing rapid re-entry.
  • Bank at Mor Ul Rek (via the TzHaar dungeon next to Zulrah's lair) or use the Fairy Ring code BJS for nearby banking.
  • Ironmen should note that Zulrah drops the toxic blowpipe components needed to craft the blowpipe themselves — important for Ranged training.
  • Do not forget to pick up all scale drops — at 100+ scales per kill this is a significant GP source.
  • After learning the rotations, aim to switch attack styles faster — reducing transition time from 3 seconds to 1 second adds several kills per hour.
