OSRS How to Complete Lost City Quest Guide 2026

Quest Guide Dramen Staff Intermediate Updated 2026

Lost City is one of OSRS's most important quests — completing it unlocks access to Zanaris (the Lost City) and lets you craft the Dramen Staff, which is required to use Fairy Rings across the entire game. This guide provides a complete step-by-step walkthrough including requirements, the Tree Spirit boss fight, and tips for low-level players.

Recommended Before Starting

  • Bring 15–20 food (lobsters or better recommended).
  • Bring prayer potions if using Protect from Melee (very helpful for the boss).
  • Have a ranged or magic weapon option — the Tree Spirit is weak to fire magic.
  • Complete the quest quickly — no particularly long travel sections required.

Complete Quest Walkthrough

  1. Start the Quest

    Head to Lumbridge Swamp (south of Lumbridge castle). Find a group of adventurers in the swamp — they can be found at the south-east corner near the swamp. Talk to any of them about Zanaris. They will mention they heard about it from a Leprechaun.

  2. Talk to the Leprechaun

    Find the Leprechaun hiding up a tree nearby. He will not be immediately visible — use the "search" option on trees in the area near the adventurers. The Leprechaun tells you about the Dramen Tree in Entrana Dungeon and warns about the Tree Spirit that guards it.

  3. Travel to Entrana

    Entrana is accessed via the Port Sarim docks, north of Draynor Village. Take the boat to Entrana. Important: You cannot bring any weapons or armour onto Entrana — the monks will confiscate them at the gangplank. Bring only food and a knife.

  4. Enter the Dungeon on Entrana

    On Entrana, head north-east to the dungeon entrance. The dungeon is marked by a cave entrance. Inside you will find zombies, greater demons, and other monsters — you cannot bring armour, so avoid combat as much as possible. Navigate to the Dramen Tree (the large glowing tree at the back of the dungeon).

  5. Fight the Tree Spirit

    When you attempt to cut the Dramen Tree, the Tree Spirit (level 101) will appear and attack you. This is the main challenge of the quest. See the Tree Spirit section below for fight tips.

  6. Cut a Dramen Branch

    After defeating the Tree Spirit, use your knife (or any slashing weapon) on the Dramen Tree to cut a Dramen Branch. This is a key item you carry in your inventory.

  7. Craft the Dramen Staff

    Use the Dramen Branch on a crafting surface or simply "use" it (with knife in inventory) to carve the branch into a Dramen Staff. This can be done anywhere.

  8. Quest Complete

    With the Dramen Staff in your possession, the quest is complete. You now have access to Zanaris (equip the staff and enter the shed in the Lumbridge Swamp) and all Fairy Rings across Gielinor.

Tree Spirit Boss Fight Tips

The Tree Spirit is the main combat challenge of Lost City. It is a level 101 monster that uses Melee attacks. You cannot bring armour to Entrana, making this fight significantly harder than it would be at a normal combat location.

Tree Spirit Stats

  • Combat Level: 101
  • Hitpoints: 120
  • Attack Style: Melee only
  • Weakness: Fire Magic (Fire Strike, Fire Bolt, Fire Blast, etc.)
  • Max Hit: ~12 (relatively low for its level, but you have no armour)

Recommended Fight Strategies

  1. Magic Attack (Best Method): Bring a staff with fire runes already set up. Ironmen can use Fire Strike (level 13 Magic) safely. Cast fire spells — the Tree Spirit is weak to fire magic and takes noticeably higher damage.
  2. Protect from Melee (Level 43 Prayer): If you have 43 Prayer, activate Protect from Melee before entering the dungeon. This eliminates virtually all damage from the Tree Spirit and makes the fight trivial regardless of your offensive method.
  3. Ranged Attack: If you have a bow and arrows (arrows are not counted as weapons by Entrana monks), you can safely range the Tree Spirit from a distance. Bronze to mithril arrows work fine.
  4. Melee Weapons found in Dungeon: There are melee weapons found on the ground within the Entrana dungeon itself (combat knife, battleaxe). You can pick these up and use them against the Tree Spirit without violating Entrana's weapon-free rule.

Using the Dramen Staff — Fairy Rings

The Dramen Staff is the key to the Fairy Ring network, one of the most powerful transportation systems in OSRS. Fairy Rings appear across the entire game world and allow instant teleportation to dozens of locations — many of which are not accessible by any other means.

  • Equip the Dramen Staff to use any Fairy Ring in the game.
  • After completing Lumbridge & Draynor Elite Diary, you no longer need the staff — just use any Fairy Ring freely.
  • Fairy Ring code BKP takes you to Zulrah, AKQ to the Fairy Godfather, and dozens of others cover all major areas.
  • The main Fairy Ring hub is in Zanaris — accessible by equipping the Dramen Staff and entering the shed in Lumbridge Swamp.
  • Keep a spare Dramen Branch in your bank — you can carve another staff any time if yours is lost or dropped.

Quest Rewards

  • 3 Quest Points
  • Access to Zanaris (The Lost City)
  • Ability to craft and use the Dramen Staff
  • Access to all Fairy Rings across Gielinor
  • Access to the Zanaris marketplace (including various rare item shops)
