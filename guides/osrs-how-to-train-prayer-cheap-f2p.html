OSRS How to Train Prayer Cheap F2P 2026

Skill Training Prayer F2P Updated 2026

Prayer is one of the most expensive skills in OSRS to train, but with the right strategy you can significantly cut costs — especially as a free-to-play player. This guide explains all the cheap Prayer training methods available in F2P, including big bone grinding, the F2P altar, the Ectofuntus method, and how to compare cost per XP across different bone types.

Prayer Training Basics

In OSRS, Prayer is trained primarily by burying or offering bones. The XP you receive depends on the type of bone and the method used. There are three ways to gain XP from bones:

  1. Burying — Simply right-click and bury bones. Gives base XP only. Most inefficient method.
  2. Using at a Gilded Altar (Members only) — Gives 3.5× the base XP when both incense burners are lit. Best XP per bone for members.
  3. Using at the Ectofuntus (F2P accessible) — Gives 4× the base XP. Best XP per bone in F2P but requires grinding bones into bonemeal first.

The Ectofuntus — Best F2P XP Per Bone

The Ectofuntus is a large altar located north of Port Phasmatys. Using it requires completing the Ghosts Ahoy quest (which is a Members quest, but F2P players can access the altar itself through a loophole). The Ectofuntus gives 4× base bone XP but requires grinding bones into bonemeal using the Bone Grinder in the basement.

Ectofuntus Method Step-by-Step

  1. Collect Big Bones from Hill Giants or purchase from the Grand Exchange.
  2. Travel to Port Phasmatys (use ectophial or walk through Morytania).
  3. Use the Bone Grinder in the dungeon below the Ectofuntus to grind bones into bonemeal.
  4. Fill a bucket with slime from the green slime pool in the same dungeon.
  5. Offer the bonemeal and slime bucket at the Ectofuntus altar for 4× XP.
  6. Repeat — collect Ectotokens as a bonus reward.

The Ectofuntus is slow due to the bone-grinding step, but delivers outstanding XP per bone. For F2P players with limited GP, using Big Bones at the Ectofuntus is the most cost-efficient Prayer method available.

Farming Big Bones from Hill Giants

If you want to train Prayer cheaply without spending GP, you can kill Hill Giants for their Big Bones. This is slower but essentially free.

Hill Giant Locations (F2P)

  • Edgeville Dungeon — Best location. Enter via the door near the Edgeville bank. Low competition off-peak. Hill Giants respawn quickly.
  • Varrock Sewers — Accessible from Varrock. Slightly more spread out than Edgeville.
  • Resource Area (Wilderness) — Requires Wilderness traversal. Not recommended for F2P due to PK risk.

At Edgeville Dungeon, you can expect roughly 60–80 Big Bones per hour, depending on combat level and world population. Each Big Bone is worth ~180 GP at the Grand Exchange, meaning you are also making a small profit while training Prayer.

Important Prayer Level Milestones

  • Level 25: Unlock Protect from Magic (useful for combat).
  • Level 31: Unlock Protect from Missiles.
  • Level 37: Unlock Protect from Melee — the most important prayer in F2P PvP and PvE.
  • Level 43: Unlock all three overhead protection prayers. This is the most commonly targeted F2P milestone.
  • Level 55: Unlock Smite (drains opponent's prayer in PvP).
  • Level 70: Unlock Piety (Members only — excellent combat boost).
  • Level 99: Prayer cape. Most F2P players stop at level 43 or 55 due to cost.

F2P Prayer Training Tips

  • Always use the Ectofuntus over burying bones — it is 4× more efficient per bone.
  • Kill Hill Giants in Edgeville Dungeon for free Big Bones if you are low on GP.
  • Level 43 (Protect from Melee) is the most useful milestone for combat — many F2P players stop here.
  • Collect Ectotokens from Ectofuntus offerings — they can be exchanged for Prayer equipment.
  • Pray drain rates mean you should not waste Prayer potions at low levels — level up combat stats first.
  • If you become a member, use a Gilded Altar with both burners lit for the best XP rates in the game.
