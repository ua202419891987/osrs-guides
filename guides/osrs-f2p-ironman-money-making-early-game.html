F2P Ironman Challenges

As an F2P Ironman, you cannot buy or sell items on the Grand Exchange. Every gold piece must be earned through direct NPC sales or alching. This means you need to focus on methods that either produce items with high alch values or items that NPCs pay well for.

The key difference between a regular F2P player and an F2P Ironman is efficiency. You can't just flip items or buy supplies — you must gather and process everything yourself. Early game priorities should be earning enough GP for essential supplies like runes, food, and equipment upgrades.

Cowhide Processing Method

Killing cows for cowhides is the most reliable early-game money maker for F2P Ironmen. Cowhides can be tanned into leather at the Al Kharid tanner for just 1 GP each, and the resulting leather can be sold to NPCs or used for Crafting XP.

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Go to the cow pen near Lumbridge or the one north of Al Kharid
  2. Kill cows and pick up cowhides (and bones for Prayer XP)
  3. Bank the cowhides — the Lumbridge bank chest is closest
  4. Once you have a full inventory, go to the Al Kharid tanner (Ellis)
  5. Tan cowhides into leather for 1 GP each
  6. Sell leather to the Al Kharid general store or use for Crafting

Spinning Flax into Bowstrings

Flax can be picked in the fields south of Seers' Village. While this is technically a members' area, F2P Ironmen who have completed the necessary steps can access limited spinning content. For pure F2P, spinning wool into balls of wool is the closest equivalent.

For F2P Ironmen, the best spinning method is collecting wool from sheep and spinning it on the Lumbridge spinning wheel. Each ball of wool can be sold or used to string amulets, which increases their alch value significantly.

Rune Essence Mining

Mining rune essence is a classic F2P money-making method. After completing the Rune Mysteries quest, you can mine rune essence in the mine south of Varrock. Pure essence is not available in F2P, but regular rune essence can still be sold to NPCs or used for Runecrafting.

For Ironmen, the best use of rune essence is to craft it into runes rather than selling it raw. Air runes are the most efficient for F2P players since the Air Altar is closest to Varrock. However, the GP/hr from selling crafted runes to NPCs is lower than cowhide processing.

Low-Level Crafting for GP

Crafting is one of the most versatile skills for Ironman money making. Even at low levels, you can create items that have decent alch values. Here are the best early Crafting methods for F2P Ironmen:

Level 1–16: Leather Items

Craft leather gloves, boots, and cowls from tanned leather. These provide modest alch values but give consistent Crafting XP. Hard leather bodies at level 28 are the real moneymaker.

Level 16–40: Jewelry (Requires Gold)

Gold rings, necklaces, and amulets can be made at the Al Kharid furnace. Gold bars are smelted from gold ore, which can be mined at the Crafting Guild (requires 40 Crafting and 40 Mining) or in the Al Kharid mine.

Level 40+: Gem Cutting & Jewelry

Once you can cut sapphires and emeralds, add them to gold jewelry for significantly higher alch values. A sapphire amulet alchs for over 800 GP — a massive upgrade from basic items.

Ironman Tips & Priorities

  • ✅ Prioritize cowhides first — they're the fastest GP with zero requirements
  • ✅ Always pick up bones — bury for Prayer XP, you'll need it later
  • ✅ Train Crafting alongside money making — process raw materials into finished goods
  • ✅ Save nature runes for alching — don't sell them; high alch is your main GP source
  • ❌ Don't sell raw materials to general stores — process them first for 5–10x more value
  • ❌ Don't skip Rune Mysteries — essence mining is a decent backup method
  • ✅ Get 55 Magic ASAP — High Level Alchemy transforms your money-making potential
