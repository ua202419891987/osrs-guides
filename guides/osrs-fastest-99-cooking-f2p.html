OSRS Fastest 99 Cooking Guide F2P 2026

🍳 Skill Training 🆓 F2P + Members Updated 2026

Cooking is OSRS's most popular 99 — it's fast, cheap (sometimes profitable), and the cape looks great. This guide covers the fastest path from level 1 to 99 Cooking in 2026 for both F2P and members players.

💡 2026 fastest path: Use Wines of Zamorak (1–35), then Karambwans (35–99). This gets you to 99 in approximately 30–40 hours of active play.

Levels 1–30: Getting Started Fast

Level 1–15: Shrimp and Anchovies

Buy raw shrimp and anchovies from the GE. Cook them on any range. Use the Lumbridge castle range — it has a 0% burn rate for early fish. This gets you to level 15 in about 10 minutes.

Level 15–30: Trout

Raw trout is cheap and plentiful. Cook at Lumbridge range. By level 25 you'll almost never burn trout here. You need about 1,000 trout to reach level 30.

Levels 30–60: Consistent Progress

Jugging Wine (Level 35) — Fastest F2P Method

Making Jugs of Wine by combining grapes with jugs of water is one of the fastest XP methods available. No fire or range required — you can do it anywhere. Each jug gives 200 XP.

Expect 400,000–500,000 XP/hr using this method. From 35 to 99, you'd need about 3,000 jugs of wine per hour session.

Salmon (Level 30–60)

An alternative to wine-making. Raw salmon is cheap and cooks quickly. Use the Rogues' Den range (underground) for 0% burn rate — it requires level 50+ Cooking and Thieving to access, but significantly reduces waste.

Levels 60–99: The Fastest Methods

⭐ Karambwans — Absolute Fastest (Level 30+, Requires Quest)

📊 XP: 900,000–1,000,000/hr ⚡ Req: Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest + Level 30 💰 Cost: ~10–30M total for 99

Cooking karambwans is the single fastest Cooking XP in the game. Each karambwan gives 190 XP, and after completing Tai Bwo Wannai Trio, you can cook them one at a time with a specific clicking technique that yields massive XP rates.

Karambwan Cooking Technique:

  1. Have raw karambwans in inventory
  2. Click the range/fire to open cooking dialog
  3. Click "Cook 1" and immediately click the range again
  4. This creates a near-instant cooking loop with almost no delay

⚠️ 2026 Note: This method has been confirmed to still work. You need to be on a low-ping world for maximum efficiency.

Sharks (Level 80+) — Cheaper Option

Sharks give 210 XP each and cooking losses are minimal. At 80+ Cooking you burn very few sharks. This is a slower but more economical path if you don't want to spend on karambwans.

F2P route to 99 takes significantly longer (~60–80 hours) compared to members, but costs very little and can even be slightly profitable if you buy/sell carefully.

💡 Best F2P tip: Use the Lumbridge castle range — it has a 5% reduced burn rate compared to regular fires, which saves you time and resources.

Profitable Cooking Methods

While not the fastest, these methods let you earn GP while training Cooking:

  • Karambwans — raw vs cooked price difference is often profitable at high Cooking levels
  • Jugs of Wine — grapes + jugs of water = wine, often small profit depending on GE prices
  • Anglerfish (level 84) — cooked anglerfish is worth more than raw in 2026

Pro Tips for 99 Cooking

  • ✅ Always use Gauntlets (from Swan Song quest) — reduces burn rate significantly from level 80+
  • ✅ Rogues' Den range has 0% burn rate for many fish types
  • ✅ Buy raw fish in bulk (5,000+) for better GE prices
  • ✅ Check GE margins — sometimes buying cooked is cheaper than buying raw + cooking
  • ✅ Cooking cape perk: 0% burn rate on all food! Worth getting early if you plan to cook a lot
