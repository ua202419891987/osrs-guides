OSRS 1-99 Thieving Guide for Ironman 2026

🎯 Skill Training 🔨 Ironman Updated 2026

Thieving is crucial for ironmen — it provides coins, gems, and supplies you can't trade for. This guide gives you the most efficient path from 1 to 99 Thieving specifically optimized for ironman accounts in 2026.

Why Ironmen Need Thieving

Unlike main accounts, ironmen can't buy supplies from other players. Thieving provides:

  • Coins — from pickpocketing NPCs
  • Gems — for crafting amulets and jewelry
  • Seeds — from Master Farmers (crucial for Herblore)
  • Runes & herbs — from various stalls and NPCs
  • Rogues' equipment — doubles coin drops from pickpocketing

Rogues' equipment (obtained from Rogues' Den) is the single most impactful gear for ironman Thieving — always prioritize getting the full outfit.

Levels 1–55: Early Game Thieving

Level 1–5: Men and Women in Lumbridge

Pickpocket men and women in Lumbridge. This takes about 5 minutes. Don't overthink it.

Level 5–25: Cake Stall in Ardougne

Stealing from the cake stall in East Ardougne gives food that helps sustain you while thieving. Useful for ironmen who need supplies early.

Level 25–45: Fruit Stall in Hosidius

Fruit stall stealing in Hosidius is excellent for ironmen — it gives fruit for Farming training (Farming contracts) and decent XP. Requires 15% Hosidius favour.

Level 45–55: Blackjacking (Pollnivneach)

Blackjacking bandits in Pollnivneach is excellent XP for its level requirement. Requires partial completion of The Feud quest. Stun a bandit with blackjack, pickpocket twice, repeat.

Levels 55–99: Pyramid Plunder

📊 XP: 150,000–280,000/hr (level dependent) ⚡ Req: Level 21 Thieving + Icthlarin's Little Helper quest 🗺️ Location: Sophanem (Pyramid Plunder minigame)

Pyramid Plunder is the fastest Thieving XP in OSRS from level 71 onwards, and decent from level 55. Complete Icthlarin's Little Helper to access Sophanem.

How Pyramid Plunder Works:

  1. Enter the pyramid and race to the highest available room
  2. Loot urns, sarcophagi, and golden chests in each room
  3. You have 5 minutes per run — maximize rooms looted
  4. Higher Thieving = higher rooms = more XP per run

💡 Ironman tip: Pyramid Plunder drops pharaoh's sceptre — a great teleport item for ironmen. Keep any you find.

Alternative: Knights of Ardougne (Level 55+)

📊 XP: 200,000–250,000/hr ⚡ Req: Level 55 Thieving + Ardougne Diary (medium) 🗺️ Location: East Ardougne Market

Pickpocketing Knights of Ardougne with the Medium Ardougne Diary complete gives a 10% increased success rate, making this very AFK-friendly. Wear Dodgy necklaces to avoid stuns.

This method also provides solid coin income for ironmen — useful for training Prayer (cannonballs) or buying supplies from NPC shops.

Key Ironman Rewards from Thieving

  • 💎 Gems from HAM members (level 15+) — for crafting amulets
  • 🌿 Seeds from Master Farmers (level 38+) — ranarr seeds for Prayer potions
  • 🏃 Rogues' outfit (from Rogues' Den) — doubles coin pickpocket drops
  • 🏺 Pharaoh's sceptre (Pyramid Plunder) — powerful teleport item
  • 💰 Dodgy necklace components — reduce stun frequency while pickpocketing
