OSRS How to Get Graceful Outfit — Full Guide 2026

🎽 Gear Unlock 🏃 Agility Updated 2026

The Graceful Outfit reduces run energy drain by 30% (full set) and is essential for any serious OSRS player. Here's exactly how to get the complete set as fast as possible in 2026.

What Is the Graceful Outfit?

The Graceful Outfit is a 6-piece set obtained by trading Marks of Grace at the Agility Shop in Burthorpe. It provides:

  • 10% run energy restoration rate per piece (up to 60% with full set)
  • Full set bonus: restores run energy 30% faster and reduces weight by 25kg
  • Essential for herb runs, bossing, and any activity requiring movement

How to Earn Marks of Grace

Marks of Grace spawn randomly on Rooftop Agility courses as you complete laps. Higher-level courses give more marks per hour.

At Seers' Village (30 marks/hour average), it takes approximately 8–10 hours of Agility training to collect all marks for the full set.

Fastest Strategy to Get Graceful Outfit

  1. Train Agility to level 60 (minimum) for Seers' Village Rooftop course
  2. Do Seers' Village course exclusively — don't waste time on lower courses
  3. Pick up every Mark of Grace (they disappear after 10 minutes)
  4. Buy pieces in priority: Cape → Boots → Gloves → Hood → Top → Legs (expensive pieces last)
  5. With full set, your run energy will recover so fast you'll rarely need to walk

💡 2026 Tip: Enable the "Drop to Grace" plugin on RuneLite to auto-highlight Marks of Grace on the minimap — you'll never miss one.

Recoloring Graceful

You can recolor your Graceful Outfit by completing specific Achievement Diaries and trading with the Agility Shop. Colors include Hosidius (green), Arceuus (purple), Lovakengj (dark), Piscarilius (yellow), and more.
