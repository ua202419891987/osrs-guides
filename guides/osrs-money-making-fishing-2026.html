Low Level Fishing Money (Level 1–50)

Trout & Salmon (Level 20–50)

While trout and salmon aren't hugely profitable (about 30–40 GP each raw), they're excellent for early game when combined with Cooking training. Fish at Barbarian Village or Lumbridge river.

Lobsters at Karamja (Level 40)

Lobsters are the classic early money maker. Each raw lobster is worth around 200 GP. Fish at Musa Point on Karamja and use the deposit box to bank efficiently. Expect ~250 lobsters per hour = ~50,000 GP/hr.

Mid Level Fishing Money (Level 50–70)

Swordfish (Level 50)

Swordfish sell for 350–450 GP each and can be caught alongside tuna (worth ~90 GP). Best locations: Musa Point (Karamja) or Fishing Guild (requires level 68 Fishing).

Monkfish (Level 62 — Requires Swan Song Quest)

Monkfish are the best mid-level fishing money maker. They sell for ~500 GP each, and the Piscatoris Fishing Colony bank is very close to the fishing spot. Expect 300–350 monkfish per hour = 150,000–175,000 GP/hr.

💡 Complete Swan Song — it's a short members quest that unlocks Piscatoris. Totally worth it for the fishing access.

High Level Fishing Money (Level 70–99)

Anglerfish (Level 82 — Requires Fishing Guild Access)

Anglerfish are a premium food item used for PvP and endgame PvE. They sell for 1,200–1,500 GP each. Fish at Port Piscarilius (requires 100% Piscarilius favour) using sandworms as bait.

Best Overall: Karambwans (Level 65)

📊 Profit: ~200,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: Level 65 Fishing + Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest 🗺️ Location: Tai Bwo Wannai, Karamja

Karambwans are a dual-eat food used heavily in PvP and bossing, keeping demand extremely high. Each raw karambwan sells for 600–800 GP.

Requirements:

  • Level 65 Fishing
  • Completed Tai Bwo Wannai Trio quest
  • Karambwan vessel + raw karambwanji as bait

Steps:

  1. Complete Tai Bwo Wannai Trio (short quest)
  2. Get your Karambwan vessel from Tiadeche
  3. Fish at the karambwan fishing spot on Karamja
  4. Bank at Shilo Village (requires Shilo Village quest) or fairy ring

Infernal Eels — Best AFK Fishing Money

📊 Profit: ~120,000–160,000 GP/hr ⚡ Req: Level 80 Fishing + Firemaking 80 🗺️ Location: Mor Ul Rek (TzHaar City)

Infernal eels are cracked open with a hammer to yield Tokkul, Onyx bolt tips, and Lava scale shards. This is a true AFK method — you can leave it running while doing other things and still earn 120,000–160,000 GP/hr.

💡 2026 Update: Onyx bolt tip prices remain stable around 3,000–4,000 GP each. The occasional drop makes this method very rewarding passively.

Ironman Fishing Tips

Ironmen should prioritize Monkfish and Karambwans for self-sustaining food supplies. Anglerfish are the endgame goal — start unlocking Piscarilius favour early.

  • ✅ Monkfish are your best early-mid game food source
  • ✅ Karambwans for PvP and Zulrah (once you unlock it)
  • ✅ Infernal Eels provide Tokkul for buying onyx — crucial for jewelry crafting
