How to Unlock Dinosaur Hunting

⚡ Req: Bone Voyage quest🦖 Hunter Level: Varies by dinosaur🗺️ Location: Fossil Island — Swamp and Volcano

Dinosaur hunting is unlocked by completing Bone Voyage quest (which grants access to Fossil Island). No additional quests are required beyond this. Simply travel to Fossil Island and head to the appropriate hunting area.

Unlock Steps:

  1. Complete Bone Voyage quest (requires 100 kudos from Varrock Museum)
  2. Travel to Fossil Island via the barge north of Digsite
  3. Head to the Tar Swamp (east side of Island) for lower-level dinosaurs
  4. Head to the Volcanic Mine area (north side) for higher-level dinosaurs

Gear and Inventory Setup

  • ✅ Bring traps of appropriate type (box traps, net traps based on creature)
  • ✅ Bring axe and pickaxe for navigating jungle/volcano obstacles
  • ✅ Bring food and anti-poison — some areas have aggressive creatures
  • ✅ Graceful outfit recommended for running between trap spots

Loot & Profit from Fossil Island Hunting

Birdhouses (passive alongside hunting) provide valuable nests with seeds and rings. Salamanders can be sold in bulk. Herbiboar (level 80 Hunter) provides valuable herbs.
