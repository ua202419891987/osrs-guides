OSRS How to Fight Corporeal Beast — Loot Guide 2026

👹 Boss Guide💰 High ValueUpdated 2026

The Corporeal Beast is one of OSRS's highest-HP bosses (2,000 HP) and drops the coveted Elysian, Spectral, and Arcane spirit shields. This guide covers everything you need to know to start killing Corp in 2026.

Gear Setup for Corporeal Beast

Melee Setup:

  • Weapon: Zamorakian hasta (spear) or Dragon hunter lance — spears deal full damage to Corp; other weapons deal 50% less
  • Helm: Slayer helmet (i) or Serpentine helm
  • Body: Karil's top or Blessed d'hide body
  • Legs: Karil's skirt or Verac's plateskirt
  • Boots: Primordial boots or Dragon boots
  • Gloves: Barrows gloves
  • Amulet: Amulet of fury / Torture
  • Ring: Berserker ring (i)
  • Cape: Fire cape / Infernal cape

💡 CRITICAL: Only spears deal full damage to Corp. Using any other weapon type reduces damage by 50%, effectively doubling the time per kill.

Killing Strategy

For Teams (3–5 Players):

  1. All players use spears (Zamorakian hasta minimum)
  2. One player focuses on the Dark Energy Core — hit it whenever it spawns
  3. Use Protect from Magic + Piety (flick if possible)
  4. Drink super restores when stats get drained
  5. Corp respawns after about 60 seconds — keep kills flowing

💡 Solo method: High-level players (90+ combat stats) use Dragon Warhammer specs → Arclight → Dragon hunter lance. Average kill time: 4–8 minutes. Corp is a marathon boss — patience and persistence pay off with sigil drops worth hundreds of millions.
