Flipping Basics: Buy Low, Sell High

Flipping means exploiting the gap between an item's buy price and sell price on the Grand Exchange. This gap exists because players set their offers slightly above or below market value.

Key Terms:

  • Buy limit — maximum amount of an item you can buy every 4 hours
  • Margin — the difference between lowest sell price and highest buy price
  • Instabuying — offering 5–10% above market to buy instantly
  • Instaselling — offering 5–10% below market to sell instantly

Basic Margin Check Process:

  1. Buy 1 of the item at a high price (instabuy) — this reveals the real sell price
  2. Immediately sell that 1 item at a low price (instasell) — this reveals the real buy price
  3. Calculate the margin: sell price minus buy price
  4. If margin is positive and >1%, it's worth flipping

How to Find Profitable Flip Margins

Manual Method (In-Game):

Use the margin check process above on any item you're interested in. Items that fluctuate throughout the day (like supplies used in bossing) tend to have the best margins.

Best Item Categories for Flipping:

  • Potions (Prayer potions, Super restores) — high volume, consistent demand
  • Food (Sharks, Anglerfish) — daily demand from PvMers
  • Runes (Nature, Law, Blood runes) — very high volume
  • Ammo (Broad bolts, Adamant darts) — slayer supply demand
  • Ores and Bars (Runite ore, Adamantite bars) — stable mid-volume

Advanced Flipping Strategies

Time-Based Flipping:

Prices shift throughout the day. Items used in bossing (potions, food) tend to be cheapest in the morning and most expensive during peak hours (evenings US time). Buy in the morning, sell in the evening.

Update Flipping:

When Jagex releases game updates, certain items spike dramatically. If a new boss drops a specific ammo type, that ammo price often jumps 50–200% within hours. Watch the OSRS news and position yourself before the update goes live.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Don't flip high-risk items without research — some items crash suddenly
  • ❌ Don't ignore buy limits — you can only buy so much every 4 hours
  • ❌ Don't rush — set your sell price and wait; impatient selling kills margins
  • ❌ Don't flip with all your gold at once — spread capital across 3–5 items
  • ✅ Always do a fresh margin check before committing large capital
