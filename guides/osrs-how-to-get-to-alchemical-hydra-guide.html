⚔️ Boss Guide 💀 Slayer 🐉 Hydra Updated 2026

Slayer & Quest Requirements

The Alchemical Hydra has strict requirements that gate access to high-level players only:

Mandatory Requirements:

  • 95 Slayer — the single biggest gate; cannot be boosted
  • Active Hydra Slayer task — assigned by Konar quo Maten
  • Dragon Slayer II — required for access to Karuulm Dungeon

Strongly Recommended:

  • 90+ Ranged — Hydra is primarily a ranged fight
  • 90+ Defence — needed to survive hits
  • 74 Herblore — for anti-venom+ potions

How to Get to Karuulm Dungeon

Karuulm Dungeon is located on Mount Karuulm in the Zeah region. There are several ways to reach it:

Once at Mount Karuulm, enter the dungeon entrance on the mountainside. Navigate through the dungeon (lower level contains drakes, wyrm, and basilisk knights) and take the elevator down to the Hydra area. The Alchemical Hydra lair is at the far end of the lower dungeon level.

Boots Requirement & Gear

The dungeon floor has hot vents that deal periodic damage if you're not wearing appropriate footwear. You must wear one of the following:

  • Insulated boots — dropped by wyrm in the dungeon; free but requires a drop
  • Dragon boots — provides the protection and melee strength bonus
  • Primordial boots — best in slot for the fight with floor protection
  • Boots of brimstone — Hydra-specific boots with magic defence bonus
  • Granite boots — budget option with floor protection

Recommended Inventory:

  • Ranged weapon (Twisted bow or Bow of Faerdhinen)
  • Ranged ammo (Dragon arrows or Amethyst arrows)
  • Anti-venom+ (4)
  • Saradomin brews (4–6)
  • Super restores (4–6)
  • Ranged potion / Divine ranging potion
  • Extended anti-fire potion

Hydra Fight Mechanics

The Alchemical Hydra has 4 phases, each changing its combat style. You must use the correct protection prayer and attack style for each phase:

PhaseHydra StylePray AgainstYour Attack StyleHP Threshold
1 (Green)RangedProtect from MissilesMelee (crush)100%–75%
2 (Blue)MagicProtect from MagicRanged75%–50%
3 (Red)RangedProtect from MissilesMelee (crush)50%–25%
4 (Gray)MagicProtect from MagicRanged25%–0%

Special Attacks to Watch For:

  • Lightning — blue electric circles appear on the ground; avoid them or take heavy damage
  • Poison pools — green poison splashes on the ground; walk away from them
  • Auto-attack change — Hydra occasionally switches attack style mid-phase; watch for the green/red projectile color

At each phase transition, the Hydra moves to the next vent. Use this brief window to eat food and reposition.

Hydra Tips

  • ✅ Use the correct attack style each phase — wrong style means massively reduced damage
  • ✅ Learn the lightning patterns — most deaths come from standing in lightning
  • ✅ Bring anti-venom+ — Hydra poisons frequently and it stacks with venom
  • ✅ Use a Twisted bow or Bow of Faerdhinen — ranged is essential for phases 2 and 4
  • ❌ Don't fight without boots — the floor damage adds up fast and drains your food
  • ❌ Don't tunnel vision on DPS — survival matters more than kill speed when learning
  • ✅ Get tasks from Konar — she's the only Slayer master who assigns Hydra tasks
