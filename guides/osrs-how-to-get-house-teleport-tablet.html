OSRS How to Get House Teleport Tablet 2026

Construction Teleports Quality of Life Updated 2026

House Teleport Tablets are one of the most useful consumable items in OSRS — they teleport you directly to your Player-Owned House without needing to be on the Arceuus spellbook. This guide explains the Construction level required, how to create tablets using a lectern, the efficient butler method, and all the uses for house teleport tablets in endgame play.

What is a House Teleport Tablet?

A House Teleport Tablet (also called a "Teleport to House" tablet) is a one-use item that teleports you to your Player-Owned House when crushed. Unlike the Teleport to House spell (which requires being on the Arceuus spellbook at level 40 Magic), the tablet works from any spellbook and any Magic level — making it invaluable for accounts that switch spellbooks frequently.

  • Works from any spellbook (Standard, Lunar, Ancient, Arceuus).
  • Useful for rushing to your house's portal nexus, fairy ring, or pool of refreshment.
  • Can be made by any player with a sufficiently levelled Construction and a Lectern in their POH.
  • Can also be made in a friend's or open world house if their lectern is available.
  • Very cheap to mass produce with the butler method — estimated 150–200 GP per tablet.

Construction Level Requirements

To make House Teleport Tablets, you need a Lectern in your Study room. Different lectern types unlock different tablets. The basic "Teleport to House" tablet requires a Marble Lectern or higher.

How to Make House Teleport Tablets

Making tablets requires soft clay and the appropriate magic runes. The process:

  1. Obtain soft clay — buy from Grand Exchange or make by using a bucket of water on clay rocks.
  2. Enter your Player-Owned House and go to the Study room with the Lectern.
  3. Right-click the Lectern and select "Study".
  4. Select "Teleport to House" from the menu.
  5. Each tablet uses 1 soft clay + runes for the Teleport to House spell (1 law rune + 1 earth rune).
  6. Click "Make X" to create as many as your inventory allows.

Butler Method — Fastest Tablet Making

The Butler method is the most efficient way to mass-produce tablets. A butler (hired via the Servant's Moneybag in your POH) will fetch noted soft clay from your bank while you continuously make tablets at the lectern. This allows you to make tablets non-stop without having to bank yourself.

Butler Method Setup

  1. Hire a Butler (level 40 Construction) or Demon Butler (level 50 Construction) via your Servant's Moneybag. Demon Butler is faster.
  2. Put a large stack of coins and noted soft clay in your bank.
  3. Enter your POH and stand at the Lectern.
  4. When your soft clay runs low, send the butler to the bank ("Speak to butler, Fetch from bank, Soft clay, 26").
  5. While the butler is away, continue making tablets with remaining clay.
  6. When the butler returns, they will un-note the clay and hand it to you automatically.
  7. Immediately start making more tablets and send the butler to fetch again.

Best Uses for House Teleport Tablets

The real power of House Teleport Tablets comes from what is inside your house. Here are the key reasons to use them frequently:

  • Pool of Refreshment (Level 65 Construction): Restores all stats, hitpoints, and special attack energy. Replace prayer potions when bossing by teleporting to your house between attempts.
  • Ornate Rejuvenation Pool (Level 90 Construction): Restores prayer as well as all the above — the ultimate house pool. Extremely powerful for boss prep.
  • Fairy Ring + Spirit Tree combo: Use a house tablet to reach your POH, then use the Fairy Ring or Spirit Tree to reach nearly any location in the game. Covers almost every boss and skilling location.
  • Portal Nexus (Level 72 Construction): Teleports to up to 12 different locations from your house. Makes your POH a global hub for travel.
  • Spellbook Altar: Switch spellbooks without going to Lunar Isle or Arceuus — crucial for accounts that switch regularly.
  • Jewellery Box: Access all jewelry teleports (ring of wealth, amulet of glory, etc.) from your house.

House Teleport Tablet Tips

  • Always keep 500+ house tablets in your bank — running out mid-session is frustrating and costly in time.
  • Set your house to Rimmington if you want to use world portal outside your house (useful for clan activities).
  • Set your house to a city with a convenient bank for the fastest overall utility (Rimmington bank area).
  • Use "Teleport to house (outside)" option to access the house portal from outside — faster than going in and out.
  • The ornate pool + POH Fairy Ring combination is considered one of the most powerful QoL setups in OSRS for active PvM players.
  • Soft clay can be gathered free from the Port Phasmatys soft clay mine (infinite supply with clay mining) — useful for Ironmen.

Getting to Level 40 Construction Fast

If you do not yet have level 40 Construction, here is the fastest way to reach it:

  1. Purchase oak planks from the Grand Exchange (or regular planks for early levels).
  2. Build and remove oak chairs in your parlour room — each chair gives 120 XP and is removable immediately.
  3. You need approximately 950 oak planks to reach level 40 from level 1.
  4. At ~3,000 GP per plank, total cost to level 40 is approximately 2.8M GP.
  5. Once level 40, build the Study room and Oak Lectern — you are ready to make tablets.
