OSRS How to Get to Thermonuclear Smoke Devil Guide 2026

👹 Boss Guide💨 Slayer BossUpdated 2026

The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil is a Slayer boss requiring level 93 Slayer. This guide covers how to reach the boss, gear requirements, and how to kill it efficiently in 2026.

Requirements

⚡ Slayer Level: 93🗺️ Location: Smoke Devil Dungeon, Stronghold Slayer Cave🧩 Required: Facemask or Slayer helmet (to breathe)

To fight the Thermonuclear Smoke Devil, you need:

  • Level 93 Slayer — one of the highest Slayer requirements in the game
  • Facemask / Nosepeg / Slayer helmet — the dungeon has dust that damages you without protection
  • No quest required
  • Combat level 100+ recommended

How to Get to the Thermonuclear Smoke Devil

Location: Stronghold Slayer Cave

  1. Use Fairy Ring DKS → this teleports you to the entrance of the Stronghold Slayer Cave (west of the Teleport spot, slightly north of the Fairy Ring)
  2. Alternatively: use the Stronghold Slayer Cave teleport (obtained from Slayer points or the Slayer ring)
  3. Enter the cave and go north past the Abyssal Demons and Nechryaels
  4. The Thermonuclear Smoke Devil is in the far northeastern corner of the cave
  5. The boss room is a separate instance — right-click the entrance to "Peek" (see if it's occupied) or "Enter" to fight

💡 Fastest route: Fairy Ring DKS → run north-west into Stronghold Slayer Cave → run north through the cave all the way to the Smoke Devil area (behind the regular Smoke Devil spawn area).

Gear Setup

Ranged Setup (Recommended):

  • Slayer helmet (i) — for 15% damage bonus on Slayer task
  • Ava's assembler / Accumulator
  • Necklace of anguish → Amulet of fury
  • Rune crossbow or Dragon hunter crossbow
  • Anti-dragon shield (not needed for Smoke Devil, but useful in Stronghold)
  • God d'hide or Karil's armour

💡 Smoke Devil has low Magic defence, but Ranged is still the most convenient due to range distance and ammo type availability. Use Ruby bolts (e) for DPS.
